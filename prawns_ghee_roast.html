Prawns Ghee Roast

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 dried red chilies (soaked in water)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp jaggery (or brown sugar)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Spice Paste
- In a blender, combine soaked dried red chilies, ginger-garlic paste.
- turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and a little water. Blend to make a smooth paste.
Step 2: Sauté the Spices
- Heat ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Once they splutter.
- add the prepared spice paste to the pan and sauté for 3-4 minutes until the oil separates from the spices.
Step 3: Cook the Prawns
- Add prawns to the pan and stir well, coating them in the spice mixture.
- Cook for 4-5 minutes until the prawns turn pink and are cooked through.
Step 4: Add Tamarind and Sweetener
- Add tamarind paste and jaggery to the pan.
- Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend and the prawns to absorb the sweet and tangy flavors.
Step 5: Adjust Consistency
- If the dish is too dry, add a little water to adjust the consistency. Stir well to combine.
Step 6: Final Touches
- Once the prawns are well-coated with the ghee and spices, remove the pan from the heat.
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
Step 7: Serve
- Serve the Prawns Ghee Roast hot with steamed rice, roti, or as a side dish with your favorite meal.
