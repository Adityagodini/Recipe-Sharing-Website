No Bake Mango Cheesecake

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g digestive biscuits (or graham crackers)
  • 75g melted butter
  • 250g cream cheese (softened)
  • 200ml whipping cream (chilled)
  • 200g mango puree (fresh or canned)
  • 100g powdered sugar
  • 2 tsp gelatin (or agar-agar for vegetarian)
  • 2 tbsp warm water (to dissolve gelatin)
  • Fresh mango slices for topping (optional)





Recipe

Step 1: Make the Biscuit Base
- Crush biscuits into fine crumbs and mix with melted butter.
- Press into the base of a springform pan. Refrigerate for 15 minutes to set.
Step 2: Prepare the Cheesecake Filling
- Dissolve gelatin in warm water and let it cool slightly.
- Whip the chilled cream until soft peaks form.
- In a separate bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and mango puree until smooth.
Step 3: Combine and Set
- Mix the dissolved gelatin into the mango-cheese mixture.
- Gently fold in the whipped cream. Pour over the chilled base and smooth the top.
Step 4: Chill
- Refrigerate for at least 6 hours or overnight until fully set.
Step 5: Garnish and Serve
- Top with fresh mango slices or a thin layer of mango puree.
- Slice and serve chilled for a refreshing tropical treat!
