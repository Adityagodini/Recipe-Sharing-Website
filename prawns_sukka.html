Prawns Sukka

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit lengthwise)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • 1 tbsp coconut (grated, optional)

Recipe

Step 1: Sauté the Aromatics
- Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and sauté for a minute.
- Add chopped onions and green chilies. Cook until the onions turn golden brown.
- Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
Step 2: Prepare the Spices
- Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2 minutes.
Step 3: Cook the Prawns
- Add prawns to the pan and cook on medium-high heat for about 5-7 minutes until they turn pink and tender.
- Stir occasionally to ensure the prawns are well-coated with the spices.
Step 4: Add Tamarind and Coconut
- Add tamarind pulp and cook for another 3-4 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
- If you like, you can also add grated coconut for extra flavor and richness.
Step 5: Final Touches and Garnish
- Sprinkle garam masala powder and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
Step 6: Serve
- Serve hot with steamed rice, chapati, or as an appetizer!
