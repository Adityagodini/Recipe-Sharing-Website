Eggless Carrot Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg powder (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup grated carrots (about 2 medium-sized carrots)
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 1/4 cup raisins (optional)
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat Oven
- Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease and line a cake pan.
Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients
- In a bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon powder, nutmeg powder, and salt.
Step 3: Mix Wet Ingredients
- In another bowl, whisk together sugar, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract until smooth.
Step 4: Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients
- Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and stir until well combined.
Step 5: Add Carrots and Nuts
- Fold in the grated carrots, walnuts, and raisins (if using) into the batter.
Step 6: Bake
- Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top.
- Bake for 35–40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Step 7: Cool and Serve
- Let the cake cool completely before slicing.
- Optionally, you can frost it with cream cheese frosting for extra indulgence!
