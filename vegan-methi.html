Vegan Methi

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups fresh methi (fenugreek) leaves, chopped
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil (preferably coconut oil for a vegan touch)

Recipe

Step 1: Clean the Methi
- Wash the methi leaves thoroughly and chop them finely.
- Optionally sprinkle some salt and set aside for 10 mins, then squeeze out the bitter water.
Step 2: Fry the Potatoes
- Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
- Add diced potatoes and fry on medium heat till golden and half cooked.
Step 3: Add Spices
- Add turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
Step 4: Add Methi Leaves
- Add chopped methi to the pan and stir well.
- Cover and cook for 7â€“10 minutes until potatoes are fully cooked and methi is tender.
Step 5: Serve Hot
- Serve hot with vegan roti, rice, or any bread of your choice. A simple, healthy, and hearty dish!
