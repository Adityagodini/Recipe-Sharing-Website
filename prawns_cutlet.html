Prawns Cutlet

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g prawns (peeled, deveined, and finely chopped)
  • 2 large potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (for coating)
  • 1 egg (beaten, for coating)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Prawns
- Heat a little oil in a pan and saut√© the chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste until fragrant.
- Add the chopped prawns and cook until they turn pink and are fully cooked. Let it cool down.
Step 2: Mix the Ingredients
- In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked prawns, mashed potatoes, red chili powder, turmeric powder.
- garam masala powder, cumin powder, black pepper, and salt. Mix well to form a dough-like mixture.
Step 3: Shape the Cutlets
- Divide the mixture into small portions and shape them into oval or round cutlets, pressing gently to form a flat shape.
Step 4: Coat the Cutlets
- Dip each cutlet into the beaten egg, ensuring it's fully coated.
- Then roll it in the breadcrumbs, pressing gently so they stick well to the cutlet.
Step 5: Fry the Cutlets
- Heat oil in a frying pan on medium heat.
- Once hot, fry the cutlets until golden brown and crispy on both sides, around 3-4 minutes per side.
- Drain excess oil on a paper towel.
Step 6: Serve
- Serve the Prawns Cutlet hot with green chutney or tomato ketchup as a snack or appetizer.
