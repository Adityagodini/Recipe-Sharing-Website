Sev Puri

INGREDIENTS

  • 10-12 puris (crisp, round puris)
  • 1/2 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cucumber, finely chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 tbsp tamarind chutney
  • 1 tbsp green chutney (mint-coriander)
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Sev (crunchy chickpea noodles) for garnish
  • Pomegranate seeds for garnish (optional)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Base
- Arrange the puris on a serving plate or tray.
- Spoon a small amount of mashed potatoes on top of each puri.
- Add a few pieces of chopped onions, tomatoes, and cucumber on each puri.
Step 2: Add the Spices
- Drizzle each puri with tamarind chutney and green chutney according to taste.
- Sprinkle chaat masala, cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt over the puris.
Step 3: Garnish & Serve
- Generously top each puri with sev for a crunchy texture.
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and pomegranate seeds for color and flavor.
- Add a squeeze of lemon juice over the top for a fresh, tangy flavor.
- Serve immediately as a delightful street food snack!
Bonus Tips:
- To add extra spice, you can drizzle some more green chutney or sprinkle more red chili powder.
- Make sure to serve Sev Puri immediately after assembling, as the puris can become soggy if left for too long.
- For an extra crunch, you can add some finely crushed papdi (crispy flour crackers) on top.
