Eggless Banana Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar or jaggery powder
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt or curd
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts or choco chips (optional)





Recipe

Step 1: Preheat and Prep
- Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease and line a loaf or round cake pan.
Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients
- In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
Step 3: Combine Wet Ingredients
- In another bowl, mix oil, sugar, yogurt, vanilla, and mashed bananas until smooth.
Step 4: Combine Wet and Dry
- Gradually fold in the dry mixture into the wet ingredients.
- Mix just until combined. Do not overmix.
Step 5: Add Nuts or Chips
- Fold in chopped walnuts or choco chips if using.
Step 6: Bake
- Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
- Bake for 30–40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
Step 7: Cool and Serve
- Let it cool for 10 minutes in the pan, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.
