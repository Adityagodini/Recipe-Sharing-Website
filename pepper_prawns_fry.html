Pepper Prawns Fry

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 1 tbsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit lengthwise)
  • 1 tbsp curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Recipe

Step 1: Marinate the Prawns
- In a bowl, combine prawns with turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt.
- Add ginger-garlic paste and mix well. Let the prawns marinate for 15-20 minutes.
Step 2: Tempering
- Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
- Add chopped onions and green chilies. Saut√© until the onions become golden brown.
Step 3: Fry the Prawns
- Add the marinated prawns to the pan and cook on medium-high heat for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
- Once the prawns are half-cooked, add black pepper powder and stir well to coat the prawns with the peppery spice.
Step 4: Final Touches
- Continue cooking the prawns for another 3-4 minutes until they are fully cooked and the masala has thickened.
- Add a squeeze of lemon juice for a tangy kick and mix well.
Step 5: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish the Pepper Prawns Fry with fresh coriander leaves.
- Serve hot with steamed rice or naan and enjoy the peppery, spicy goodness!
