Eggless Orange Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup oil (vegetable or sunflower oil)
  • 3/4 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1 tbsp orange zest
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice




Recipe

Step 1: Preheat Oven
- Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake tin.
Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients
- In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
Step 3: Prepare Wet Mixture
- In another bowl, whisk together sugar, oil, orange juice, orange zest, vanilla extract,
- and vinegar or lemon juice.
Step 4: Combine
- Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients. Mix until smooth and lump-free.
Step 5: Bake
- Pour the batter into the prepared cake tin. Bake for 30–35 minutes or
- until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
Step 6: Cool and Serve
- Let the cake cool completely before slicing. Enjoy the fresh citrus flavor in every bite!
