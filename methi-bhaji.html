Methi Bajji

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups fresh methi (fenugreek) leaves, cleaned and chopped
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1–2 green chilies, chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds





Recipe

Step 1: Prep Methi
- Wash methi leaves thoroughly to remove bitterness. Chop and set aside.
Step 2: Temper the Pan
- Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
Step 3: Sauté Aromatics
- Add chopped onions, green chilies, and garlic. Sauté until onions turn soft and light golden.
Step 4: Add Methi
- Add chopped methi leaves and turmeric. Stir well. Cook on medium flame for 5–6 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Step 5: Adjust Taste
- Add salt and continue cooking until methi is well done and dry. No water should remain.
Step 6: Serve Hot
- Serve with chapati, roti, or as a side dish with dal-chawal. Healthy and flavorful!
