Matki Usal

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup moth beans (matki), soaked overnight and sprouted
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp hing (asafoetida)
  • 1/2 tsp jaggery (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1/2 cup water

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Moth Beans
- Soak the moth beans overnight and allow them to sprout for 1-2 days.
Step 2: Heat Oil and Temper Spices
- In a pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and hing. Let them splutter.
Step 3: Add Onions, Ginger, and Green Chilies
- Add chopped onions, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Saut√© until the onions become translucent.
Step 4: Add Tomatoes and Spices
- Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
- Cook until the tomatoes soften and the oil separates.
Step 5: Add Sprouted Moth Beans
- Add the sprouted moth beans to the pan and mix well with the masala.
Step 6: Add Water and Simmer
- Add 1/2 cup water and cook the mixture on low heat for 10-15 minutes until the beans are tender and the flavors are absorbed.
Step 7: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with chapati or rice.
