Prawns Biryani

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 1 1/2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large onion (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit lengthwise)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp fresh mint leaves (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

    • Whole spices:

  • 1 bay leaf, 2 cloves, 2 green cardamom pods,
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick, 1-star anise, 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water

Recipe

Step 1: Cook the Rice
- In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add whole spices and salt.
- Add the basmati rice and cook until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
Step 2: Marinate the Prawns
- In a bowl, combine prawns with yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder.
- coriander powder, cumin powder, cardamom powder, lemon juice, and salt. Let the prawns marinate for 15-20 minutes.
Step 3: Prepare the Biryani Base
- Heat ghee and oil in a large pan or pressure cooker.
- Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown. Remove half of the fried onions for garnishing.
Step 4: Cook the Tomatoes and Spices
- To the same pan, add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies. Sauté until fragrant.
- Then add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
Step 5: Add Marinated Prawns
- Add the marinated prawns to the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes until they are half cooked.
- Add fresh coriander and mint leaves, then remove from the heat.
Step 6: Layer the Biryani
- In a large pot, layer the cooked prawns with rice.
- Sprinkle the reserved fried onions, mint, and coriander leaves on top.
- Drizzle with a little ghee for extra flavor.
Step 7: Steam the Biryani
- Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid or seal it with dough.
- Cook on low heat (dum) for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the rice to cook fully.
Step 8: Serve
- Once done, gently fluff the biryani with a fork. Serve hot with raita or a salad.
