No Bake Oreo

INGREDIENTS

  • 28 Oreo cookies (regular size)
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 200ml heavy cream (chilled)
  • 200g sweetened condensed milk
  • 100g cream cheese (softened, optional for richness)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Extra crushed Oreos for garnish







Recipe

Step 1: Make the Base
- Crush 18 Oreos into fine crumbs using a food processor or ziplock and rolling pin.
- Mix with melted butter and press into the base of a springform pan. Chill for 15 minutes.
Step 2: Prepare the Cream Mixture
- Whip the chilled heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
- In a separate bowl, beat condensed milk, cream cheese (if using), and vanilla until smooth.
- Fold in the whipped cream gently.
Step 3: Assemble the Cake
- Roughly crush the remaining 10 Oreos and fold into the cream mixture.
- Pour this over the chilled crust and smooth the top.
Step 4: Chill to Set
- Refrigerate the cake for at least 6 hours or overnight until firm.
Step 5: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with more crushed Oreos. Slice and serve chilled. Enjoy your creamy, no-bake Oreo delight!
