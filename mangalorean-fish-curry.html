Mangalorean Fish Curry

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g fish (Kingfish, Mackerel, or any firm fish)
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut (fresh or desiccated)
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste or 1 small piece of tamarind soaked in warm water
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required to make the curry
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Coconut Paste
- In a blender, grind the grated coconut with a little water to make a smooth coconut paste. Set aside.
Step 2: Sauté the Base
- Heat coconut oil in a pan or clay pot. Add curry leaves and green chilies and sauté for a few seconds.
- Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown and soft.
- Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
Step 3: Cook the Tomatoes and Spices
- Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
- Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder.
- Stir well and cook the spices for 2-3 minutes to bring out their flavor.
Step 4: Add Coconut Paste
- Add the prepared coconut paste to the pan and mix well with the onion-tomato-spice mixture.
- Add tamarind paste or soaked tamarind to the curry. Mix again.
- Add enough water to make the curry at your desired consistency. Bring it to a boil.
Step 5: Add the Fish
- Add the fish pieces to the curry, ensuring they are submerged in the gravy. Season with salt to taste.
- Simmer the curry on low-medium heat for 10-12 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through.
- Avoid stirring too much to prevent breaking the fish pieces.
Step 6: Garnish and Serve
- Once the fish is cooked and the curry has thickened slightly, remove the curry from the heat.
- Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
- Serve hot with steamed rice or neer dosa. Enjoy your delicious Mangalorean Fish Curry!
