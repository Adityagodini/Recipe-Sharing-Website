Kache Keerai

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup fresh spinach (palak) or amaranth leaves (thandu keerai), finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp grated coconut
  • 1 small green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp sesame oil or coconut oil

Recipe

Step 1: Clean and Chop Greens
- Wash the keerai thoroughly in running water 2â€“3 times.
- Drain completely and finely chop the greens.
Step 2: Mix Ingredients
- In a bowl, combine chopped keerai, grated coconut, green chili (if using), and salt.
Step 3: Add Flavors
- Drizzle with sesame or coconut oil and squeeze in the lemon juice.
- Toss everything gently until well mixed.
Step 4: Serve Fresh
- Serve immediately as a healthy side dish with rice and dal or curd rice.
