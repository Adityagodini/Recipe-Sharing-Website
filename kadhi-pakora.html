Kadhi Pakora

INGREDIENTS

  • For Pakoras (Fritters):
  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Water (as required to make a batter)
  • Oil (for frying)
  • For Kadhi:
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Pakoras (Fritters)
- In a bowl, mix besan, chopped onion, green chilies, cumin seeds, carom seeds, red chili powder,
- add turmeric powder, and salt.
- Gradually add water and make a thick batter.
- Heat oil in a pan and drop spoonfuls of the batter into the hot oil.
- Fry the pakoras until golden brown and crispy.
- Remove and set aside on paper towels to drain excess oil.
Step 2: Prepare the Kadhi
- In a mixing bowl, whisk yogurt and besan together to form a smooth mixture.
- Add water to the mixture and whisk again to make a thin batter. Keep aside.
- Heat oil in a pot. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, dried red chilies, and asafoetida. Let them splutter.
- Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for a minute until fragrant.
- Pour in the yogurt-besan mixture and bring it to a boil, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming.
- Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
- Lower the heat and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to combine and the kadhi to thicken.
Step 3: Combine Pakoras with Kadhi
- Add the fried pakoras to the kadhi and simmer for another 5-7 minutes.
- The pakoras will soak up the flavors of the kadhi.
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
- Serve hot with rice, naan, or roti. Enjoy your delicious Kadhi Pakora!
