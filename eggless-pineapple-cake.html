Eggless Pineapple Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice (fresh or canned)
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped pineapple (fresh or canned)
  • Whipped cream or buttercream for frosting
  • Pineapple slices and cherries for decoration



Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Batter
- In a bowl, whisk together condensed milk, pineapple juice, oil, and vanilla extract until smooth.
Step 2: Sift Dry Ingredients
- In a separate bowl, sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
- Gently fold dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
Step 3: Add Pineapple
- Mix in the chopped pineapple. Do not overmix.
Step 4: Bake the Cake
- Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a round cake tin.
- Pour the batter and bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
Step 5: Cool and Frost
- Allow cake to cool completely. Slice the cake horizontally if layering.
- Apply whipped cream or buttercream between the layers and on the outside.
Step 6: Decorate
- Decorate with pineapple slices, cherries, or as desired.
- Chill for at least an hour before serving.
