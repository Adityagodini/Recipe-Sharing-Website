Vanila Sponge Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp lemon zest (optional)






Recipe

Step 1: Preheat the Oven
- Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch round cake pan or line it with parchment paper.
Step 2: Prepare the Dry Ingredients
- In a small bowl, sift together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
Step 3: Beat the Eggs
- In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs and granulated sugar until.
- the mixture becomes light and fluffy, about 5-7 minutes. You can use an electric mixer for this step.
Step 4: Add Wet Ingredients
- Stir in the melted butter, vanilla extract, and lemon zest (if using), mixing until fully combined.
Step 5: Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients
- Gradually fold the sifted dry ingredients into the wet ingredients using a spatula or spoon.
- Be gentle to avoid deflating the batter. If the batter is too thick.
- you can add the milk a little at a time until the batter reaches a smooth consistency.
Step 6: Bake the Cake
- Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan, smoothing the top with a spatula.
- Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
Step 7: Cool the Cake
- Once the cake is baked, remove it from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
- Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
Step 8: Serve and Enjoy
- Once the cake has cooled, serve it as is or decorate it with frosting, whipped cream, or fresh fruit for extra flavor.
