No Bake Coconut Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups desiccated coconut
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup crushed digestive biscuits or graham crackers
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Extra desiccated coconut (for coating)







Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Base Mixture
- In a large bowl, mix crushed biscuits, desiccated coconut, melted butter, condensed milk, vanilla extract, and salt.
- Mix until a sticky dough forms.
Step 2: Shape the Cake
- Press the mixture evenly into a greased or lined cake pan, smoothing the top with a spatula or spoon.
Step 3: Chill the Cake
- Refrigerate for at least 3â€“4 hours or until firm and set.
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
- Once set, unmold the cake and sprinkle extra desiccated coconut on top. Slice and serve chilled.
