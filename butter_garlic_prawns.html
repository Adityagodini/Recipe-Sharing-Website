Butter Garlic Prawns

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 6-8 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1/4 cup white wine (optional)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce (optional)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Prawns
- Clean and devein the prawns, ensuring they are dry before cooking.
- You can also marinate them with a pinch of salt, black pepper, and lemon juice for 15 minutes if you prefer.
Step 2: Heat Butter and Oil
- In a large pan, heat butter and olive oil over medium heat until the butter melts completely.
Step 3: Sauté Garlic and Ginger
- Add the chopped garlic and grated ginger to the pan, sautéing for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
- Be careful not to burn the garlic.
Step 4: Cook the Prawns
- Add the prawns to the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes, turning them until they turn pink and are fully cooked.
- If using, pour in white wine and soy sauce for added depth of flavor.
Step 5: Season and Finish
- Sprinkle red chili flakes, black pepper, and salt over the prawns.
- Stir to ensure the prawns are evenly coated with the butter and garlic sauce.
Step 6: Garnish and Serve
- Once cooked, remove the prawns from the heat and squeeze fresh lemon juice over them.
- Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and a sprinkle of lemon zest.
- Serve immediately with a side of toasted bread or steamed rice for a complete meal!
