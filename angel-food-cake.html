Angle Food Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup cake flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 12 large egg whites, at room temperature
  • 1 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp almond extract (optional)
  • 1/4 cup warm water

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat the Oven
- Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Do not grease the angel food cake pan.
- The ungreased pan helps the cake rise properly.
Step 2: Prepare the Dry Ingredients
- Sift the cake flour and half of the sugar (3/4 cup) together into a bowl, and set aside.
Step 3: Beat the Egg Whites
- In a large clean bowl, beat the egg whites using an electric mixer on medium speed until they become frothy.
- Add cream of tartar and salt, and continue beating until soft peaks form.
Step 4: Gradually Add Sugar
- Gradually add the remaining sugar (3/4 cup) to the egg whites, one tablespoon at a time, while continuing to beat at high speed.
- Beat until the egg whites form stiff, glossy peaks.
Step 5: Add Vanilla and Almond Extracts
- Gently fold in the vanilla extract and almond extract (if using) using a spatula or spoon.
Step 6: Fold in the Flour
- Sift the remaining flour mixture over the egg whites in batches.
- Gently fold the flour into the egg whites using a spatula, taking care not to deflate the batter.
Step 7: Transfer to the Pan
- Pour the batter into the ungreased angel food cake pan, smoothing the top with a spatula.
Step 8: Bake the Cake
- Bake the cake for 35-40 minutes or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Step 9: Cool the Cake
- Once baked, invert the pan immediately and allow the cake to cool completely while hanging upside down.
- This helps the cake maintain its height and airy texture.
Step 10: Remove the Cake from the Pan
- After the cake has cooled completely, use a knife to gently loosen the edges from the pan.
- Carefully lift the cake out of the pan and transfer it to a serving plate.
Step 11: Serve
- Slice and serve the light and airy angel food cake on its own, or with fresh berries and whipped cream.
