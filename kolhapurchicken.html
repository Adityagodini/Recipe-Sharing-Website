-cumin seeds, dried red chilies, black peppercorns, cinnamon stick, cloves, and.

- Let the spices cool, and then grind them into a fine powder using a grinder or mortar-pestle.
- In the same pan, add 1 tbsp oil and saut√© the ginger-garlic paste until it turns fragrant and golden brown.
