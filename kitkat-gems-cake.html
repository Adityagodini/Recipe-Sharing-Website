Kitkat Gems Cake

INGREDIENTS

    For the Cake Base:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

    • For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 1 cup heavy cream

    • For the Decoration:

  • 1 pack of Kit Kat bars
  • 1 cup Gems (colored chocolate candies)
  • Whipped cream (for piping, optional)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Cake Base
- Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a 9-inch round cake pan.
- In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
- Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until combined.
- Gradually add the boiling water to the mixture, stirring until smooth. The batter will be thin.
- Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30-35 minutes or.
- until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let the cake cool completely.
Step 2: Prepare the Ganache
- Chop the dark chocolate and place it in a heatproof bowl.
- Heat the heavy cream in a saucepan over medium heat until it begins to simmer. Do not let it boil.
- Pour the hot cream over the chocolate and let it sit for 2-3 minutes.
- Stir the mixture until smooth and glossy. Allow the ganache to cool slightly.
Step 3: Assemble the Cake
- Once the cake has cooled, slice it horizontally into two layers.
- Place the first layer of cake on a serving platter and spread a thin layer of ganache on top.
- Add the second layer of cake and cover the entire cake with the remaining ganache, smoothing it with a spatula.
Step 4: Decorate the Cake
- Break the Kit Kat bars into individual fingers and arrange them vertically around the sides of the cake.
- gently pressing them into the ganache.
- Pour the Gems candies on top of the cake in a decorative pattern.
- Optionally, pipe some whipped cream on the top edges for extra decoration.
Step 5: Chill and Serve
- Refrigerate the cake for at least 2 hours to set the ganache and allow the decorations to hold.
- Once set, slice and serve chilled. Enjoy the colorful and delightful Kit Kat & Gems Cake!
Serve hot with buttered toast, roti, or paratha, and enjoy your delicious Egg Bhurji!
