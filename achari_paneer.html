Achari Panner

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 onions (finely sliced)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (well whisked)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp cashew nuts (soaked and blended to paste)
  • 1 tsp poppy seeds (optional, soaked and ground)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Fresh coriander or mint for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Base
- Heat ghee in a pan. Add cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and bay leaf.
- Add sliced onions and sauté till golden brown.
- Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till raw smell fades.
Step 2: Make the Masala
- Add turmeric, coriander powder, and a little water.
- Add cashew-poppy paste and mix well. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
- Lower the flame and add whisked yogurt slowly, stirring constantly.
Step 3: Add Paneer and Cream
- Add paneer cubes and mix gently. Cook for 5 minutes on low heat.
- Pour in the fresh cream and stir. Sprinkle garam masala.
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with fresh mint or coriander. Serve hot with naan, pulao, or paratha.
