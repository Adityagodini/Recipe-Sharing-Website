Panchmel Dal Rajasthani

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 cup moong dal (yellow split lentils)
  • 1/4 cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 1/4 cup chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 cup masoor dal (red lentils)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 2 cups water (adjust for consistency)

Recipe

Step 1: Cook the Lentils
- In a pressure cooker, add all the lentils (moong dal, toor dal, chana dal, urad dal, and masoor dal) with 2 cups of water.
- Cook for about 3-4 whistles or until soft and mushy. Set aside.
Step 2: Temper the Spices
- In a pan, heat ghee or oil. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
Step 3: Sauté the Aromatics
- Add the chopped onions, green chilies, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.
Step 4: Add Tomatoes and Spices
- Add the chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala.
- Cook until the tomatoes turn soft and oil separates.
Step 5: Combine the Lentils and Spice Mixture
- Add the cooked lentils to the pan and mix well. Add salt to taste.
- Let the dal simmer for about 10-15 minutes, adding water if needed to reach desired consistency.
Step 6: Final Touch
- Once the dal is cooked, garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
Step 7: Serve
- Serve hot with roti, rice, or a side of pickles. Enjoy the hearty and flavorful Panchmel Dal!
