Fish Paturi

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g fish fillets (preferably hilsa or any firm fish)
  • 2 tbsp mustard paste (made with yellow mustard seeds)
  • 2 tbsp coconut paste
  • 1 tbsp green chili paste (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Banana leaves (for wrapping the fish)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit (for garnish)
  • Water as required for marination
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Fish Marinade
- Clean the fish fillets and marinate them with turmeric powder, salt, mustard paste, green chili paste.
- ginger-garlic paste, cumin powder, and a little water. Let it marinate for 30-45 minutes.
Step 2: Prepare the Mustard-Coconut Paste
- In a bowl, mix the mustard paste, coconut paste, and sugar.
- Add mustard oil and a little lemon juice to this mixture and stir well to combine.
Step 3: Wrap the Fish
- Take banana leaves and cut them into pieces large enough to wrap the fish fillets.
- Lightly heat the leaves over an open flame to make them more pliable.
- Place the marinated fish fillet on a piece of banana leaf. Spoon a generous amount of the mustard-coconut mixture over the fish.
- Carefully fold the banana leaf around the fish to create a sealed parcel.
Step 4: Steam the Fish
- Heat a steamer or a large pot with a steaming rack.
- Place the wrapped fish parcels in the steamer and steam for 20-25 minutes until the fish is fully cooked and tender.
Step 5: Serve
- Carefully open the banana leaf parcel and place the fish on a plate.
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and slit green chilies for added flavor.
- Serve the Fish Paturi with steamed rice or any side dish of your choice.
