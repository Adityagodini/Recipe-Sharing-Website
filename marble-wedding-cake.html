Marble Wedding Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • ½ cup hot water
  • Buttercream frosting or fondant for decoration

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat & Prepare
- Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line your cake pans with parchment paper.
Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients
- In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
Step 3: Cream Butter & Sugar
- In a large bowl, cream the softened butter and sugar together until light and fluffy, about 3-4 minutes.
Step 4: Add Eggs & Vanilla
- Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in the vanilla extract.
Step 5: Add Milk & Sour Cream
- Gradually add the milk and sour cream, alternating with the dry ingredients. Mix until smooth.
Step 6: Prepare the Chocolate Batter
- In a separate bowl, mix the cocoa powder with hot water to create a smooth chocolate paste.
- Take a portion of the batter (about 1/4th) and mix it with the chocolate paste until combined.
Step 7: Layer the Batters
- Spoon the plain batter and the chocolate batter into the cake pans in alternating layers.
- Use a knife or skewer to gently swirl the two batters together to create a marble effect.
Step 8: Bake the Cake
- Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
- Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
Step 9: Prepare the Frosting
- Prepare your buttercream frosting or fondant according to your preference for decorating.
Step 10: Assemble the Cake
- Once the cakes have cooled, place the first layer on a serving plate. Spread a layer of frosting evenly over the top.
Step 11: Add the Second Layer
- Place the second cake layer on top, and frost the top and sides with the buttercream or fondant.
Step 12: Final Touch
- Decorate the cake with any desired accents, such as flowers, pearls, or edible decorations for a beautiful wedding presentation.
Step 13: Serve
- Slice and enjoy the elegant marble wedding cake, a stunning centerpiece for your celebration!
