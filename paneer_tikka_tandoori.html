Panner Tikka

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1 tbsp fresh coriander (chopped, for garnish)
  • 1 onion (sliced, for garnish)
  • 1 lemon (cut into wedges, for garnish)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Marinade
- In a mixing bowl, combine yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric,
- garam masala, cumin powder, chaat masala, lemon juice, mustard oil, salt, and kasuri methi.
- Mix everything well into a smooth paste.
Step 2: Marinate the Paneer
- Add the cubed paneer to the marinade and mix gently so the paneer is fully coated.
- Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (overnight for best results).
Step 3: Grill or Bake the Paneer
- Preheat the oven or tandoor to 200°C (400°F).
- Skewer the marinated paneer cubes and place them on a baking tray lined with parchment paper, or use a grilling rack.
- Grill or bake for 20-25 minutes, turning halfway through until the paneer is golden and slightly charred on the edges.
Step 4: Garnish & Serve
- Once done, remove from the oven, and sprinkle with fresh coriander and chaat masala.
- Serve the Paneer Tikka with onion rings, lemon wedges, and a green chutney on the side.
