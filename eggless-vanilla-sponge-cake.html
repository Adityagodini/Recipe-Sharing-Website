Eggless Vanila Sponge Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt





Recipe

Step 1: Preheat the Oven
- Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line an 8-inch round cake pan.
Step 2: Mix Wet Ingredients
- In a bowl, whisk together yogurt, milk, oil, vinegar (or lemon juice), and vanilla extract.
Step 3: Combine Dry Ingredients
- In another bowl, sift flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
Step 4: Make the Batter
- Slowly fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Mix until smooth and lump-free.
Step 5: Bake
- Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
Step 6: Cool and Serve
- Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then cool fully on a wire rack. Serve as is or frost as desired.
