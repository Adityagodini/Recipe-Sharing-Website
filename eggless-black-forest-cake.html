Eggless Black Forest Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup cherry compote or tinned cherries
  • 1/2 cup sugar syrup (for soaking)
  • 2 cups whipped cream
  • Chocolate shavings (for garnish)
  • Cherries (for topping)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Batter
- Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line two 7-inch round cake pans.
- Mix yogurt, milk, oil, vinegar, and vanilla in a bowl.
Step 2: Combine Dry Ingredients
- Sift flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, sugar, and salt into another bowl.
- Fold into wet mix and combine well.
Step 3: Bake
- Divide the batter into pans and bake for 25–30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool completely.
Step 4: Soak and Layer
- Slice each cake into two horizontal layers. Sprinkle sugar syrup generously on each layer.
- Spread whipped cream and cherries between layers.
Step 5: Frost the Cake
- Cover the entire cake with whipped cream. Decorate with chocolate shavings and cherries on top.
Step 6: Chill and Serve
- Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before slicing. Serve chilled and enjoy!
