Taro Root Palak Curry

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g taro root (arbi), boiled, peeled and sliced
  • 1 bunch spinach (palak), washed and chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/4 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Water as needed

Recipe

Step 1: Prep Arbi & Palak
- Boil taro root until soft, peel and slice into rounds. Lightly sauté in a bit of oil until golden.
- Set aside. Blanch spinach in hot water for 2 minutes, drain, and puree or chop finely.
Step 2: Start Tempering
- Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add onions and green chili. Sauté till golden brown.
Step 3: Spice It Up
- Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
- Then add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook till tomatoes soften.
Step 4: Add Spinach & Arbi
- Add spinach puree/chopped leaves. Stir and cook for 3–4 mins. Add sautéed taro root. Mix gently to avoid breaking.
Step 5: Simmer & Finish
- Add a splash of water if curry looks too thick. Cover and simmer on low flame for 7–10 minutes.
- Sprinkle garam masala at the end.
Step 6: Serve with Love
- Serve hot with chapati, paratha, or even rice. A comforting and nutritious meal!
