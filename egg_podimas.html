Egg Podimas

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp ginger (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp garlic (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp fresh grated coconut (optional)
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 pinch asafoetida (hing)

Recipe

Step 1: Prep the Base
- Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
- Add cumin seeds, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Sauté for a few seconds.
Step 2: Sauté Aromatics
- Add chopped onions, green chilies, ginger, and garlic. Sauté until onions turn soft and slightly golden.
- Add turmeric powder and stir well.
Step 3: Add the Eggs
- Crack the eggs into a bowl, add salt and pepper, and beat lightly.
- Pour the eggs into the pan and stir continuously on low to medium heat.
- Scramble the eggs gently until they are fully cooked but still moist. Avoid overcooking.
Step 4: Final Touch
- Once cooked, add fresh grated coconut (if using) and chopped coriander leaves.
- Mix well and turn off the heat.
Step 5: Serve
- Serve hot with rice, rasam, sambar, chapati, or just enjoy it plain as a protein-rich snack!
