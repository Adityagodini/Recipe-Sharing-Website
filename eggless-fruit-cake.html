Eggless Fruit Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup mixed dried fruits (raisins, chopped dates, candied peel, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg powder
  • 1/2 cup oil or melted butter
  • 1 cup orange juice or apple juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice


Recipe

Step 1: Preheat Oven
- Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake tin.
Step 2: Soak Fruits
- Soak the dried fruits in orange or apple juice for at least 30 minutes.
- You can do this overnight for richer flavor.
Step 3: Mix Dry Ingredients
- In a bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and a pinch of salt.
Step 4: Prepare Wet Mixture
- In another bowl, mix sugar, oil, vanilla extract, vinegar (or lemon juice), and soaked fruits along with the juice.
Step 5: Combine & Mix
- Add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture. Stir until well combined. Fold in the chopped nuts if using.
Step 6: Bake
- Pour the batter into the greased tin.
- Bake for 35–40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
Step 7: Cool and Serve
- Let it cool completely before slicing. Serve as a festive treat or tea-time delight!
