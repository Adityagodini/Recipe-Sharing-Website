No Bake Caramel Pecan Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups crushed digestive biscuits or graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 can (400g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup caramel sauce (plus extra for topping)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)
  • Whole pecans for garnish




Recipe

Step 1: Make the Base
- Combine crushed biscuits, melted butter, brown sugar, cinnamon, and chopped pecans in a bowl.
- Mix well until the mixture resembles wet sand.
- Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan or dish to form a crust.
- Chill in the refrigerator for 20 minutes to firm up.
Step 2: Prepare the Filling
- In a mixing bowl, blend together sweetened condensed milk, caramel sauce, and vanilla extract until smooth.
- Pour this mixture evenly over the chilled crust.
Step 3: Set the Cake
- Refrigerate the cake for at least 4 hours or until itâ€™s firm and set (overnight is best).
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
- Drizzle extra caramel sauce on top.
- Add whipped cream and whole pecans as garnish if desired.
- Slice and serve chilled.
