Panner Dosa

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp oil (for cooking dosa)
  • 200g paneer (grated)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Dosa Batter
- Wash rice and urad dal thoroughly and soak them separately for about 4-6 hours.
- Soak fenugreek seeds with the urad dal for better fermentation.
- Grind the rice and urad dal into a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
- The batter should be thick, not runny.
- Add salt to the batter, mix well, and let it ferment overnight or for 8-12 hours in a warm place.
Step 2: Prepare the Paneer Filling
- Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and allow them to splutter.
- Add chopped onions, green chili, and saut√© until onions turn translucent.
- Add grated paneer, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and lemon juice.
- Stir well and cook for a couple of minutes until the paneer softens and all spices are well blended.
- Add coriander leaves and mix.
- Set the paneer filling aside.
Step 3: Cook the Dosa
- Heat a non-stick pan or griddle (tawa) over medium heat. Lightly grease it with oil.
- Pour a ladle of dosa batter onto the hot pan and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin, round dosa.
- Drizzle a little oil on the edges of the dosa and cook until the bottom turns golden brown.
- Flip the dosa carefully (optional) and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
Step 4: Add Paneer Filling
- Once the dosa is cooked, place a portion of the prepared paneer filling in the center of the dosa.
- Fold the dosa in half or roll it up, covering the paneer filling inside.
Step 5: Serve
- Serve the hot Paneer Dosa with coconut chutney, tomato chutney, or sambar for dipping.
