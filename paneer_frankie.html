Panner Frankie

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 4-5 whole wheat or refined flour wraps
  • 1 medium onion (finely sliced)
  • 1 medium tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup lettuce (shredded)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Paneer Filling
- Heat oil in a pan and add the ginger-garlic paste. Sauté for a minute.
- Add the cubed paneer and sauté until slightly golden.
- Add red chili powder, garam masala, soy sauce, tomato ketchup, and salt.
- Stir well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Set aside.
Step 2: Prepare the Wraps
- Heat the wraps or parathas on a tawa until soft and warm. Optionally, brush a little oil on them.
- Once warmed, place them on a flat surface ready for assembling the wrap.
Step 3: Assemble the Paneer Frankie
- On each wrap, spread a layer of shredded lettuce, followed by a few slices of onion, tomato, and cucumber.
- Place a generous amount of the prepared paneer filling in the center of each wrap.
- Drizzle a little lemon juice and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
Step 4: Wrap it up
- Carefully roll the wrap tightly, folding in the edges as you go, to secure the filling inside.
Step 5: Serve
- Serve the Paneer Frankie immediately with a side of chutney or dip of your choice.
