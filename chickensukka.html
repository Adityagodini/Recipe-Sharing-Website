    For Marinade:

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

    • For Gravy:

  • 2 tbsp oil (preferably coconut oil)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit lengthwise)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh grated coconut (or coconut powder)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-2 sprigs curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

üë©‚Äçüç≥Recipe

Marinate the Chicken:
- In a bowl, mix chicken with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, salt, and lemon juice.
- Let the chicken marinate for 30-60 minutes to absorb all the flavors.
Prepare the Gravy:
- Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds, let them splutter.
- Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
- Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cook for a minute until fragrant.
- Add chopped tomatoes, cook until they soften and become mushy.
Cook the Chicken:
- Add marinated chicken pieces to the pan, and cook on high heat for 3-4 minutes to seal the chicken's juices.
- Lower the heat and add cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and garam masala.
-Stir well and cook the chicken in the spices for another 5-7 minutes.
Add Coconut and Simmer:
- Add the grated coconut to the pan and stir it into the chicken.
-You can also use coconut powder if fresh coconut is not available.
- Add a little water to adjust the consistency (it should be thick).
-Let it cook on medium-low heat for 15-20 minutes, until the chicken is fully cooked and the flavors have blended.
Garnish and Serve:
- Add curry leaves and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
- Serve hot with rice, roti, or naan.
