Photo Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • 2 cups heavy cream (for frosting)
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar (for frosting)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (for frosting)
  • Photo icing sheet (printed edible image)
  • Edible glitter (optional)

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat the Oven
- Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
- Grease and line two 8-inch round cake pans with parchment paper.
Step 2: Prepare Dry Ingredients
- In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
Step 3: Prepare Wet Ingredients
- In another bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
- Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
Step 4: Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients
- Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with buttermilk, mixing until smooth.
Step 5: Bake the Cake Layers
- Pour the batter into the prepared cake pans and smooth the tops with a spatula.
- Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
- Let the cakes cool in their pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
Step 6: Prepare the Frosting
- In a chilled bowl, whip the heavy cream with powdered sugar and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
Step 7: Assemble the Cake
- Once the cakes are completely cool, spread a thin layer of frosting between the two cake layers to secure them.
- Frost the entire cake with the remaining whipped cream frosting.
Step 8: Apply the Edible Photo
- Carefully place the photo icing sheet on top of the frosted cake.
- Smooth the edges gently to ensure it adheres to the frosting.
Step 9: Final Touches
- Optionally, decorate the edges with edible glitter or other cake decorations.
Step 10: Serve
- Slice and serve the beautiful Photo Cake to your guests, perfect for special occasions like birthdays or anniversaries!
