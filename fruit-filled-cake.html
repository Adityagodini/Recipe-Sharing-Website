Fruit Filled Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • 1 cup mixed fresh fruits (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, kiwi, etc.)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 cup whipped cream or frosting
  • 1/3 cup fruit preserve (such as strawberry or raspberry jam)

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat & Prepare
- Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and line your cake pans with parchment paper.
Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients
- In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
Step 3: Cream Butter & Sugar
- In a large bowl, cream the softened butter and sugar together until light and fluffy, about 3-4 minutes.
Step 4: Add Eggs & Vanilla
- Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract. Mix well after each addition.
Step 5: Add Dry Ingredients & Milk
- Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk.
- Start and end with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.
Step 6: Bake the Cake
- Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
- or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
- Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
Step 7: Prepare the Fruit Filling
- Chop the mixed fresh fruits into small pieces. In a bowl, mix the fruits with lemon juice and fruit preserve. Set aside.
Step 8: Whip the Cream
- If using whipped cream, whip it until soft peaks form. If using frosting, prepare according to your preference.
Step 9: Assemble the Cake
- Once the cakes have cooled, place the first layer on a serving plate. Spread a layer of fruit filling evenly over the cake.
Step 10: Add the Second Layer
- Place the second cake layer on top, and frost the top and sides with whipped cream or frosting.
Step 11: Garnish with Fruit
- Garnish the cake with extra fresh fruits for a colorful and attractive presentation.
- You can also drizzle some extra fruit preserve over the top if you like.
Step 12: Serve
- Slice and enjoy this delicious fruit-filled cake, perfect for any celebration!
