Kadhai Gobi

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 medium-sized cauliflower (gobi), cut into florets
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh cilantro (coriander leaves) for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Vegetables
- Wash and cut the cauliflower into florets.
- You can blanch them in hot water for 2 minutes to soften, but it’s optional.
- Finely chop the onion, bell pepper, and tomatoes.
Step 2: Heat the Spices
- Heat oil in a pan or kadhai over medium heat.
- Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
- Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
Step 3: Add Ginger-Garlic Paste and Tomatoes
- Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
- Add the chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt.
- Cook until the tomatoes soften and release oil.
Step 4: Add the Cauliflower and Bell Pepper
- Add the cauliflower florets and chopped bell pepper to the pan.
- Mix well to coat the vegetables with the spices.
- Cook covered for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally,
- until the cauliflower becomes tender but still holds its shape.
Step 5: Garnish and Serve
- Sprinkle garam masala over the cooked vegetables and mix well.
- Garnish with fresh cilantro (coriander leaves).
- Serve hot with roti, paratha, or rice.
