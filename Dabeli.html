Prepare the Filling:

-Stir in mashed potatoes and salt.
-Cook for a couple of minutes till everything mixes well and gets aromatic.

-Spread garlic chutney on one side, sweet chutney on the other.
-Place a portion of potato masala inside.
-Top with sev, more peanuts, pomegranate, and some green chutney if you like extra zing.
