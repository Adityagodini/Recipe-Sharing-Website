Tandoori Broccoli

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 medium broccoli head (cut into florets)
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon tandoori masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or melted butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Broccoli
- Wash and cut the broccoli into florets.
- Blanch the broccoli in boiling water for 2-3 minutes,
- then immediately transfer to ice-cold water to stop cooking. Drain well.
Step 2: Prepare the Marinade
- In a mixing bowl, combine yogurt, tandoori masala, cumin powder, coriander powder,
- turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, olive oil, and salt.
- Mix well to form a smooth marinade.
Step 3: Marinate the Broccoli
- Add the blanched broccoli florets to the marinade and coat them evenly.
- Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.
Step 4: Preheat the Oven
- Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) or preheat a grill pan on medium heat.
Step 5: Cook the Broccoli
- Arrange the marinated broccoli florets on a baking tray lined with parchment paper or a grilling rack.
- Roast in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, turning once halfway through,
- or until they are slightly charred and tender. If using a grill pan, cook for about 5-7 minutes on each side.
Step 6: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with a wedge of lemon.
- Enjoy your tandoori broccoli as a snack or appetizer!
