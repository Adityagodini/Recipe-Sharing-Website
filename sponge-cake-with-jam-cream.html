Sponge Cake with Jam Cream

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup strawberry jam (or any jam of choice)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (for whipped cream)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Sponge Cake
- Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8-inch round cake pan.
- In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until creamy and light.
- Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
- In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
- Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with milk. Mix until smooth.
- Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and bake for 20-25 minutes.
- until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
- then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
Step 2: Whip the Cream
- In a chilled mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
- Be careful not to overwhip the cream. Once soft peaks form, stop whipping.
Step 3: Assemble the Cake
- Once the cake has cooled completely, slice it in half horizontally to create two layers.
- Spread a generous layer of strawberry jam (or any jam of your choice) on the bottom layer of the cake.
- Top the jam layer with a thick spread of whipped cream.
- Place the second layer of cake on top and gently press it down.
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
- Decorate the top of the cake with more whipped cream and a drizzle of jam if desired.
- Slice and serve the cake chilled, and enjoy the soft, spongy texture paired with the jam and creamy filling!
