Caramel Drip Cake

INGREDIENTS

    For the Cake Base:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

    • For the Caramel Drip:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

    • For the Frosting:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups whipped butter (room temperature)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Cake Base
- Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease and line two 8-inch round cake pans.
- In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
- In another bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
- Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
- Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with milk and sour cream. Mix until smooth.
- Divide the batter evenly between the two prepared cake pans.
- Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
- Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
Step 2: Prepare the Caramel Drip
- In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the sugar, stirring occasionally until it turns amber in color.
- Be careful not to burn it.
- Once the sugar has melted, add the butter and whisk until smooth.
- Slowly pour in the heavy cream, continuing to whisk until the mixture is fully combined.
- Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
- Let the caramel cool to room temperature before using.
Step 3: Prepare the Frosting
- In a chilled mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
- In a separate bowl, whip the room temperature butter until soft and creamy.
- Gradually fold the whipped cream into the butter, mixing gently until fully incorporated.
Step 4: Assemble the Cake
- Once the cakes are fully cooled, level them if needed, and place one cake layer on a serving platter.
- Spread a generous layer of frosting on top of the first cake layer.
- Place the second layer of cake on top and frost the entire cake with the remaining frosting.
Step 5: Add the Caramel Drip
- Carefully pour the cooled caramel sauce over the edges of the cake, allowing it to drip down the sides.
- Drizzle any remaining caramel on top of the cake.
- Optional: Garnish with some caramelized nuts, chocolate shavings, or a dusting of cocoa powder.
Step 6: Chill and Serve
- Refrigerate the cake for at least 30 minutes to set the frosting and caramel drips.
- Slice and enjoy the indulgent caramel drip cake, perfect for any special occasion!
