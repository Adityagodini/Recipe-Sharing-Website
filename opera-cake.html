Opera Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 4 large eggs, separated
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup sugar (for the coffee syrup)
  • 1/4 cup water (for the coffee syrup)
  • 2 tbsp rum (optional)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Almond Sponge Cake
- Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease and line a 9-inch rectangular baking pan.
- In a bowl, combine almond flour, all-purpose flour, and powdered sugar. Set aside.
- In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks with half of the granulated sugar until pale and creamy.
- In another bowl, beat the egg whites until soft peaks form.
- then gradually add the remaining sugar and beat until stiff peaks form.
- Gently fold the egg whites into the yolk mixture, followed by the dry ingredients.
- Lastly, fold in the melted butter and vanilla extract.
- Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until golden. Let it cool completely.
Step 2: Make the Coffee Syrup
- In a small saucepan, combine the coffee, sugar, and water.
- Heat until the sugar dissolves and the syrup thickens slightly. Remove from heat and add rum, if using. Let it cool.
Step 3: Prepare the Coffee Buttercream
- In a mixing bowl, beat the softened butter with powdered sugar until smooth and creamy.
- Gradually add the cooled coffee and continue beating until smooth. Set aside.
Step 4: Prepare the Chocolate Ganache
- In a heatproof bowl, combine the chopped chocolate and heavy cream.
- Heat in the microwave or over a double boiler until the chocolate melts and the ganache is smooth.
- Let it cool to room temperature.
Step 5: Assemble the Opera Cake
- Once the almond sponge cake has cooled, slice it into three equal layers.
- Place the first layer on a serving platter and brush with half of the coffee syrup.
- Spread a layer of coffee buttercream over the sponge.
- Place the second layer of sponge on top and repeat the process with the remaining coffee syrup and coffee buttercream.
- Finally, place the last layer of sponge on top, spread the chocolate ganache over the top, and smooth it out with a spatula.
Step 6: Chill and Serve
- Refrigerate the cake for at least 4 hours to allow the layers to set.
- Once chilled, slice and serve. Enjoy the rich, layered flavor of this classic French dessert!
