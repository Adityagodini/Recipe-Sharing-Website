Prawns Frankie

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g prawns (peeled, deveined, and chopped)
  • 2 large flour tortillas (or chapatis)
  • 1 onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 green bell pepper (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (for marinating prawns)
  • Salt to taste
  • Mint chutney (for drizzling)
  • Tomato ketchup (optional)

Recipe

Step 1: Marinate the Prawns
- In a bowl, combine prawns with yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder,.
- lemon juice, and salt. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.
Step 2: Prepare the Filling
- Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions, tomatoes, and green bell peppers until they soften.
- Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
- Add the marinated prawns and cook for 5-7 minutes until the prawns are cooked through and the mixture thickens.
Step 3: Prepare the Tortillas
- Warm the flour tortillas or chapatis in a pan for a few seconds on each side until soft and pliable.
Step 4: Assemble the Frankie
- Place a warm tortilla on a flat surface.
- Spoon the prawn mixture in the center, top with some chopped coriander leaves.
- and drizzle with mint chutney and tomato ketchup.
Step 5: Roll the Frankie
- Carefully roll the tortilla over the filling, folding the sides inward as you go, to make a wrap.
Step 6: Serve
- Serve the Prawns Frankie hot with a side of mint chutney or ketchup for dipping.
