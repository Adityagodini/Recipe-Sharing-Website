Saag Aloo

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups spinach (palak), chopped
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Spinach
- Blanch spinach in boiling water for 2 minutes, then transfer to cold water.
- Drain and blend into a coarse paste.
Step 2: Cook the Potatoes
- In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add diced potatoes and cook until golden and tender.
Step 3: Sauté the Base
- Add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chili to the same pan. Sauté till golden brown.
Step 4: Add Masala and Spinach
- Add turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Mix well. Add the spinach paste and cooked potatoes. Combine thoroughly.
Step 5: Simmer and Serve
- Cover and cook for 5-7 minutes until flavors meld. Serve hot with roti, paratha, or rice.
