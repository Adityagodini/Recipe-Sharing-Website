Fondant Theme Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups fondant (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar (for dusting)
  • Buttercream frosting (enough to cover the cake)
  • Food coloring (for decorating the fondant)
  • Theme-specific decorations (characters, logos, or edible images)

Recipe

Step 1: Bake the Cake Layers
- Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease and flour two round cake pans.
- In a bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
- Cream the softened butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
- then add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
- Stir in the vanilla extract and gradually add the dry ingredients while alternating with the milk.
- Mix until smooth.
- Divide the batter evenly between the two prepared cake pans and bake for 25-30 minutes.
- until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
- Allow the cakes to cool completely on a wire rack.
Step 2: Prepare the Buttercream
- In a large mixing bowl, beat together the butter, powdered sugar, vanilla extract,
- and a small amount of milk until smooth and fluffy.
- If necessary, add more powdered sugar to reach the desired consistency for spreading.
Step 3: Apply the Buttercream
- Once the cakes have cooled, level the tops of the cakes if necessary.
- Spread a layer of buttercream on top of one of the cakes, then place the second cake on top, creating a sandwich.
- Apply a thin crumb coat of buttercream over the entire cake and refrigerate for 30 minutes to set the base.
Step 4: Roll Out the Fondant
- Dust a clean surface with powdered sugar to prevent sticking.
- Roll out the fondant to the desired thickness (about 1/8 inch) using a rolling pin.
- Gently lift the rolled fondant and drape it over the cake.
- Smooth the fondant over the cake, gently pressing out air bubbles and wrinkles.
- Trim the excess fondant at the base of the cake.
Step 5: Decorate with Fondant
- Roll out additional fondant in different colors and cut out shapes or designs for the theme.
- For example, you can create flowers, patterns, or even themed figures like cartoon characters or logos.
- Use edible food coloring to paint or airbrush details onto the fondant, giving it a vibrant and personalized look.
Step 6: Final Touches
- Arrange the fondant decorations on the cake as desired
- and make any final adjustments to ensure the theme is visible and appealing.
- If you have an edible image or print, you can place it on top of the fondant as the centerpiece.
Step 7: Slice and Serve
- Once your fondant-themed cake is beautifully decorated, it’s ready to be sliced and served to your guests.
- Enjoy the reactions as you cut into the cake, revealing the beautiful designs and delicious layers inside!
