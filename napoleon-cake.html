Napolean Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 sheets puff pastry (store-bought or homemade)
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Puff Pastry
- Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
- Roll out the puff pastry sheets on a lightly floured surface.
- Cut each sheet into 3 even rectangles, about 4x6 inches each.
- Place the pastry rectangles on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
- Prick the surface with a fork to prevent puffing.
- Bake the puff pastry in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until golden and crispy.
- Let the pastry sheets cool completely.
Step 2: Make the Pastry Cream
- In a medium saucepan, combine the milk, heavy cream, and sugar.
- Heat over medium heat until the mixture is hot, but not boiling.
- In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, cornstarch, and vanilla extract until smooth.
- Gradually add a small amount of the hot milk mixture to the egg yolk mixture, whisking constantly to temper the eggs.
- Slowly pour the tempered egg mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining milk mixture.
- Cook over medium heat, whisking constantly, until the mixture thickens (about 5-7 minutes).
- Once thickened, remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the butter until fully incorporated.
- Let the pastry cream cool to room temperature.
Step 3: Assemble the Napoleon Cake
- Place one layer of puff pastry on a serving platter. Spread a generous amount of cooled pastry cream over the puff pastry.
- Place another layer of puff pastry on top of the cream.
- Repeat the process with the remaining layers of puff pastry and pastry cream, finishing with a top layer of puff pastry.
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
- Dust the top layer of the Napoleon cake with powdered sugar for a beautiful finish.
- Refrigerate the cake for at least 2 hours before serving to allow the flavors to meld and the cake to set.
- Slice into individual servings and enjoy the light, crispy layers of pastry and creamy filling!
