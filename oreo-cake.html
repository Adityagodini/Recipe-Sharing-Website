Oreo Cake

INGREDIENTS

    For the Cake:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 10-12 crushed Oreo cookies (for the batter)

    • For the Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2-3 tbsp milk (adjust consistency)
  • 8-10 crushed Oreo cookies (for frosting)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Cake
- Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease and line two 8-inch round cake pans.
- In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
- Add the vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and mix well.
- Gradually add the milk to the batter, mixing until smooth.
- Fold in the crushed Oreo cookies.
- Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans and bake for 25-30 minutes.
- until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
- Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
Step 2: Prepare the Frosting
- In a mixing bowl, beat the room temperature butter until soft and creamy.
- Gradually add the powdered sugar and cocoa powder, beating until smooth and fluffy.
- Stir in the vanilla extract and 2-3 tbsp of milk to achieve a spreadable consistency.
- Fold in the crushed Oreo cookies into the frosting for added texture.
Step 3: Assemble the Cake
- Once the cakes have completely cooled, place one cake layer on a serving plate.
- Spread a generous layer of the Oreo frosting over the first cake layer.
- Place the second cake layer on top and frost the entire cake with the remaining frosting.
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish the top of the cake with whole Oreo cookies for decoration.
- Refrigerate the cake for about 30 minutes to set the frosting before slicing and serving.
- Enjoy your delicious, creamy Oreo cake with friends and family!
