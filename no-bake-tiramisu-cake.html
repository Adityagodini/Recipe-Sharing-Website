No Bake Tiramisu Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g ladyfinger biscuits (savoiardi)
  • 250g mascarpone cheese
  • 200ml heavy whipping cream
  • 100g powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee (cooled)
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder (for dusting)
  • Grated dark chocolate (optional for garnish)





Recipe

Step 1: Make the Cream Mixture
- Whip heavy cream until soft peaks form.
- In another bowl, beat mascarpone, powdered sugar, and vanilla.
- Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture.
Step 2: Prepare Coffee Soak
- Mix brewed coffee and liqueur (if using) in a shallow dish.
Step 3: Assemble Layers
- Dip each ladyfinger quickly in the coffee and layer in a cake tin or dish.
- Spread half of the cream mixture on top.
- Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the remaining cream.
Step 4: Chill and Garnish
- Cover and refrigerate for at least 6 hours or overnight.
- Before serving, dust generously with cocoa powder and optionally top with grated chocolate.
