Swiss Roll

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup strawberry jam (or any jam of choice)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar (for whipped cream)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (for whipped cream)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Sponge Cake
- Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 10x15-inch jelly roll pan with parchment paper.
- In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
- In a separate large bowl, beat the eggs and sugar together until light and fluffy, about 5-6 minutes.
- Add the vanilla extract, then gradually fold in the dry ingredients. Be gentle to preserve the air in the mixture.
- Gently stir in the milk and melted butter until fully combined.
- Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread it evenly.
- Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the cake springs back when lightly touched.
- Remove from the oven and let the cake cool for 5 minutes.
- Then, invert the cake onto a clean kitchen towel dusted with powdered sugar. Peel off the parchment paper carefully.
Step 2: Roll the Cake
- While the cake is still warm, gently roll it up with the towel from one short end.
- Let the rolled cake cool completely while wrapped in the towel.
- This helps the cake maintain its shape when unrolled.
Step 3: Prepare the Filling
- In a mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream with powdered sugar and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
- Be careful not to overwhip.
Step 4: Assemble the Swiss Roll
- Once the cake has cooled completely, gently unroll it from the towel.
- Spread a thin layer of strawberry jam (or any jam of your choice) evenly over the cake.
- Spread the whipped cream filling evenly on top of the jam layer.
- Carefully roll the cake back up, this time without the towel, to form the Swiss roll.
Step 5: Garnish and Serve
- Dust the top of the Swiss roll with powdered sugar for a decorative finish.
- Slice into individual pieces and serve. Enjoy the soft, fluffy texture of the cake paired with the jam and whipped cream filling!
