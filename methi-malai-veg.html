Methi Malai Veg

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and cubed
  • 1 cup fresh methi leaves (fenugreek), chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/4 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream or cashew paste (for vegan)
  • 1/2 cup milk (or plant-based milk)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (optional, for aroma)

Recipe

Step 1: Prep the Methi
- Wash and chop the methi leaves. Lightly sauté them in a bit of oil for 2-3 minutes to reduce bitterness. Set aside.
Step 2: Sauté Base
- In a pan, heat oil or ghee. Add onions and green chilies. Cook until soft and golden.
Step 3: Add Spices
- Add ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, coriander powder. Cook until raw smell goes away.
Step 4: Creamy Touch
- Add boiled aloo cubes. Mix well. Pour in cream and milk (or cashew paste and almond milk). Stir until creamy and thick.
Step 5: Methi Time
- Add sautéed methi and mix gently. Cook on low heat for 5–7 minutes. Add garam masala and kasuri methi for aroma.
Step 6: Serve Hot
- Serve hot with naan, paratha, or jeera rice. It’s rich, herby, and delightfully veg-friendly!
