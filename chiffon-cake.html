Chiffon Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 7 large eggs, separated
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp cream of tartar

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat the Oven and Prepare the Pan
- Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C). Do not grease the chiffon cake pan.
- Set the pan aside, as the batter needs to cling to the sides for proper rising.
Step 2: Mix the Dry Ingredients
- In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt until well combined.
Step 3: Mix the Wet Ingredients
- In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, vegetable oil, water, and vanilla extract until smooth.
- Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until fully incorporated.
Step 4: Whisk the Egg Whites
- In a clean, dry bowl, beat the egg whites with cream of tartar using an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.
Step 5: Fold the Egg Whites into the Batter
- Gently fold the beaten egg whites into the batter in batches, being careful not to deflate the egg whites.
- Mix until just combined, ensuring the batter remains light and airy.
Step 6: Bake the Cake
- Pour the batter into the ungreased chiffon cake pan and smooth the top.
- Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Step 7: Cool the Cake
- Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and immediately invert the pan to cool completely.
- This prevents the cake from collapsing.
Step 8: Remove the Cake from the Pan
- After the cake has cooled completely, use a knife to gently loosen the edges of the cake from the pan.
- Carefully remove the cake from the pan and place it on a serving platter.
Step 9: Serve
- Slice and serve the fluffy and light chiffon cake on its own, or with frosting or fresh fruits of your choice.
