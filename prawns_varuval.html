Prawns Varuval

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • Fresh curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Recipe

Step 1: Heat Oil and Temper Spices
- Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.
- Add fennel seeds, mustard seeds, and curry leaves. Allow them to splutter for a few seconds.
Step 2: Sauté Onions and Tomatoes
- Add the sliced onions and sauté until they become soft and golden brown.
- Add the chopped tomatoes, and cook until they soften and turn mushy.
Step 3: Add Ginger-Garlic Paste and Spices
- Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another 2 minutes.
- Now add the turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt.
- Stir well and cook for a minute until the spices release their aroma.
Step 4: Add the Prawns
- Add the prawns to the pan and stir gently to coat them with the spice mixture.
- Cook the prawns for about 5-7 minutes until they turn pink and are cooked through.
Step 5: Simmer and Finish
- Add garam masala and lemon juice to the prawns and give it a final stir.
- Let the prawns cook on low heat for another 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
Step 6: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
- Serve hot with steamed rice or as a standalone appetizer. Enjoy the delicious, spicy prawns varuval!
