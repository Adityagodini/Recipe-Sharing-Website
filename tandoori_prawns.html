Tandoori Prawns

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 2 tbsp thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • Onion rings (for garnish)

Recipe

Step 1: Marinate the Prawns
- In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder,
- garam masala, lemon juice, vegetable oil, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt.
- Add prawns to the marinade and mix well. Ensure all prawns are coated with the marinade.
- Cover and refrigerate for at least 1-2 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld.
Step 2: Preheat the Grill or Oven
- Preheat the grill or oven to 200°C (392°F).
- If using a grill, make sure the skewers are well-oiled to prevent sticking.
Step 3: Grill the Prawns
- Thread the marinated prawns onto skewers and place them on the grill or in the oven.
- Grill the prawns for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally,
- until they are cooked through and slightly charred on the edges.
- Keep an eye on them to avoid overcooking, as prawns cook quickly.
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
- Once cooked, remove the prawns from the grill and let them rest for a minute.
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves, onion rings, and a sprinkle of kasuri methi.
- Serve hot with a side of green chutney and lemon wedges!
