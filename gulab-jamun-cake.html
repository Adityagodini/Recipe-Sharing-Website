Gulab Jamun Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup oil or melted butter
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 6-8 mini gulab jamuns (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/3 cup gulab jamun syrup (for soaking)
  • Whipped cream or condensed milk frosting (optional)
  • Chopped pistachios and rose petals (for garnish)

Recipe

Step 1: Mix the Batter
- In a bowl, whisk curd and sugar till smooth. Add oil and mix well.
- Sift in flour, baking powder, baking soda, and cardamom powder. Fold gently.
- Add milk to adjust the batter consistency — it should be smooth and pourable.
Step 2: Bake the Cake
- Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line a cake tin.
- Pour the batter into the tin and bake for 30–35 minutes or until done.
Step 3: Soak with Syrup
- Let the cake cool slightly. Poke holes and drizzle gulab jamun syrup over the cake.
Step 4: Decorate
- Frost with whipped cream or pour a layer of sweetened condensed milk.
- Place gulab jamuns on top and sprinkle chopped pistachios and dried rose petals.
Step 5: Serve
- Chill for 1–2 hours for best results. Cut into slices and enjoy this fusion delight!
