Genoise Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 6 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup warm water

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat the Oven
- Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line the bottom of a 9-inch round cake pan with parchment paper.
Step 2: Mix the Dry Ingredients
- Sift the flour and salt together into a bowl. Set aside.
Step 3: Whisk the Eggs and Sugar
- In a large mixing bowl, whisk the eggs and sugar together until the mixture is thick and pale, about 5-7 minutes.
Step 4: Add the Vanilla
- Add the vanilla extract to the egg mixture and gently fold it in.
Step 5: Fold in the Flour
- Gradually fold the sifted flour and salt mixture into the egg mixture.
- Use a gentle folding motion to avoid deflating the batter.
Step 6: Add the Butter and Water
- In a small bowl, combine the melted butter and warm water. Gently fold the butter-water mixture into the batter.
Step 7: Bake the Cake
- Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top.
- Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the cake is golden and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Step 8: Cool the Cake
- Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
Step 9: Serve
- Once the cake has cooled, you can frost it, fill it, or serve it as is.
- Genoise cake is perfect for layered cakes and desserts!
