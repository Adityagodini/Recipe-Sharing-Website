Prawns Fry Andhra

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit lengthwise)
  • 1 tbsp curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Recipe

Step 1: Marinate the Prawns
- In a bowl, add the prawns along with turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder.
- cumin powder, and salt.
- Add ginger-garlic paste and mix well. Let the prawns marinate for 15-20 minutes.
Step 2: Tempering
- Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
- Add chopped onions and green chilies. Saut√© until the onions become golden brown.
Step 3: Fry the Prawns
- Add the marinated prawns to the pan and cook on medium-high heat for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
- Once the prawns are almost cooked.
- add tamarind paste and cook for another 3-4 minutes until the prawns are fully cooked and coated with the masala.
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish the Prawn Fry with fresh coriander leaves.
- Serve hot with rice or roti and enjoy this spicy Andhra-style prawn fry!
