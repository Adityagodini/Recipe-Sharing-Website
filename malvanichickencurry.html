    For Marinade:

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Malvani masala (see below for preparation)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder

    • For Malvani Masala:

  • 2 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 5-6 dried red chilies

  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder

    • For Gravy:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • 1 cup water (for gravy consistency)

Prepare the Malvani Masala:
- Dry roast the coriander seeds, cumin seeds, dried red chilies, black peppercorns, cinnamon, cloves.
-cardamom pods, fennel seeds, and turmeric powder in a pan over low heat.
- Roast until aromatic, then allow the spices to cool down.
- Grind the roasted spices into a fine powder using a grinder or mortar and pestle.
-This is your homemade Malvani masala.
Marinate the Chicken:
- In a bowl, mix the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, Malvani masala.
-red chili powder, turmeric powder, salt, and lemon juice.
- Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes to absorb all the flavors.
Prepare the Gravy:
- Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the chopped onions until golden brown.
- Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
- Add pureed tomato and cook for 5 minutes until the oil starts separating from the mixture.
- Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook until they turn golden brown, stirring occasionally.
Cook the Chicken:
- Add the Malvani masala powder to the chicken and mix well.
-Let the chicken cook in the spices for about 10 minutes.
- Pour in the coconut milk and water, adjusting the consistency of the curry as per your preference.
- Cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
Garnish and Serve:
- Once the chicken is cooked, sprinkle garam masala and give it a final stir.
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve the Malvani chicken curry hot with steamed rice, roti, or naan.
