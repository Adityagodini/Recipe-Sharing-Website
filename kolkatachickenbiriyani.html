- In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add bay leaves, cloves, cardamoms, cinnamon, and salt.
- Add the soaked rice and cook it until 70% done (not fully cooked, as it will cook further with the chicken).

- Heat ghee in a large, heavy-bottomed pan and fry the onions until golden brown (this will take around 8‚Äì10 minutes).

-until the chicken is half-cooked and the oil starts to separate from the gravy.
Layering the Biryani:
- In a large pot, add a layer of the cooked chicken gravy at the bottom.
- Next, add a layer of the partially cooked rice over the chicken.
- Sprinkle some garam masala, chopped coriander, chopped mint leaves, kewra water, and rose water over the rice.
- Repeat the layers of chicken and rice until the pot is full.
- The top layer should be rice, garnished with the reserved fried onions and a few mint/coriander leaves.
