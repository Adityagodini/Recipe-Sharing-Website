Cheesecake Wedding Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 cups cream cheese, softened
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups sour cream
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Fresh fruit (strawberries, raspberries, blueberries) for topping (optional)
  • Mint leaves (optional for garnish)

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat & Prepare the Pan
- Preheat the oven to 160°C (325°F). Grease and line the sides of your springform pan with parchment paper.
Step 2: Make the Crust
- In a bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter.
- Stir until well mixed. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the pan to form an even crust.
Step 3: Bake the Crust
- Bake the crust in the preheated oven for about 10 minutes or until lightly golden.
- Remove from the oven and let it cool while preparing the filling.
Step 4: Prepare the Cheesecake Filling
- In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy.
- Add vanilla extract, eggs (one at a time), sour cream, heavy cream, and lemon juice. Beat until fully combined.
Step 5: Pour the Filling into the Pan
- Pour the cheesecake filling over the cooled crust in the springform pan.
- Tap the pan gently on the counter to remove air bubbles.
Step 6: Bake the Cheesecake
- Place the cheesecake in the oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the center is set but still slightly jiggly.
- Turn off the oven, crack the oven door, and let the cheesecake cool for 1 hour.
Step 7: Chill the Cheesecake
- After the cheesecake has cooled for an hour, cover it with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
- preferably overnight, to set completely.
Step 8: Prepare the Topping
- Before serving, top the cheesecake with fresh fruit such as strawberries, raspberries, and blueberries.
- Garnish with mint leaves if desired.
Step 9: Assemble the Wedding Cake
- If making a multi-tier cheesecake wedding cake, repeat the process to create multiple layers and stack them.
- Use dowels or support rods for stability between layers.
Step 10: Serve
- Slice and serve this delicious and creamy cheesecake wedding cake as the perfect sweet ending to your celebration!
