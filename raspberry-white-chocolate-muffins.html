Raspberry White Chocolate Muffins

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1/2 cup white chocolate chips

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat the Oven
- Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 12-cup muffin tin with cupcake liners.
Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients
- In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
Step 3: Mix Wet Ingredients
- In a separate bowl, combine the melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth.
Step 4: Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients
- Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing just until combined. Do not overmix.
Step 5: Fold in Milk, Raspberries, and White Chocolate
- Stir in the milk, fresh raspberries, and white chocolate chips gently, folding them into the batter without crushing the raspberries.
Step 6: Fill Muffin Tin
- Spoon the batter into the muffin liners, filling each cup about 2/3 full.
Step 7: Bake the Muffins
- Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
Step 8: Cool the Muffins
- Let the muffins cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
Step 9: Serve and Enjoy
- Enjoy these fluffy and sweet Raspberry White Chocolate Muffins as a perfect breakfast or snack!
