Jeera Aloo

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes (boiled and peeled)
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds (jeera)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-green chili paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh cilantro (coriander leaves) for garnish
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice (optional)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Potatoes
- Boil the potatoes until soft. Peel them and cut into cubes or slices based on preference.
Step 2: Heat the Spices
- Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
- Add cumin seeds (jeera) and let them splutter.
Step 3: Add Spices and Ginger-Chili Paste
- Add the ginger-green chili paste and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
- Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Stir well to combine.
Step 4: Add Potatoes and Cook
- Add the boiled potatoes to the pan and mix gently to coat the potatoes with the spices.
- Cook on low heat for 5-7 minutes, occasionally stirring to ensure the potatoes don’t stick to the pan.
Step 5: Garnish and Serve
- Sprinkle garam masala over the potatoes and mix well.
- Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
- You can also squeeze a little lemon juice on top for added freshness (optional).
- Serve hot with roti, paratha, or rice.
