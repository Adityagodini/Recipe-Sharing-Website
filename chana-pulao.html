Chana Pulao

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas (chana)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: Rinse the Rice
- Rinse the basmati rice thoroughly until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 15-20 minutes and drain.
Step 2: Temper the Spices
- Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and cloves.
- Let them splutter for a few seconds.
Step 3: Saut√© the Aromatics
- Add the sliced onions, green chilies, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Saut√© until the onions become golden brown.
Step 4: Add Tomatoes and Spices
- Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
- Stir in turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala powder. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
Step 5: Add the Chickpeas
- Add the boiled chickpeas (chana) to the pan and mix them with the spices. Stir gently to coat the chickpeas with the spices.
Step 6: Cook the Rice
- Add the soaked and drained rice to the pan and saut√© for a minute to combine the flavors.
Step 7: Add Water and Cook
- Add 2 cups of water and salt to taste. Bring the water to a boil.
- Once it boils, lower the heat, cover, and cook for about 10-12 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
Step 8: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
