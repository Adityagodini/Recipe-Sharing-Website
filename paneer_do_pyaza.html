Panner Do Pyaza

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 large onions (1 chopped, 1 cut into petals)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp kasuri methi (crushed)
  • 2 tbsp curd (whisked)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish



Recipe

Step 1: Sauté Onions
- Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
- Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
- Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till raw smell disappears.
Step 2: Cook the Masala
- Add turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder. Sauté for a minute.
- Add tomato puree and cook till oil separates.
- Mix in whisked curd and stir well, cooking on low flame.
Step 3: Add Paneer and Onion Petals
- Add paneer cubes and onion petals. Gently stir everything together.
- Cook for 4–5 minutes on low heat. Add kasuri methi and garam masala.
Step 4: Serve
- Garnish with chopped coriander. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
