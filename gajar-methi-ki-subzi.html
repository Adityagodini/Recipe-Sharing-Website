Gajar Methi Ki Subzi

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups grated carrots (gajar)
  • 1 cup fresh fenugreek leaves (methi), chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Recipe

Step 1: Heat the Oil
- Heat oil in a pan or kadai on medium heat.
- Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics
- Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
- Add green chili and ginger-garlic paste, and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
Step 3: Cook the Carrots
- Add the grated carrots to the pan and stir well to mix with the aromatics.
- Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Stir and cook for 5-6 minutes until the carrots soften.
Step 4: Add Fenugreek Leaves
- Add the chopped methi leaves to the pan and stir well.
- Cook for an additional 4-5 minutes until the methi wilts and blends with the carrots.
Step 5: Add Garam Masala
- Sprinkle garam masala powder and mix well.
- Cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
Step 6: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with fresh cilantro (optional) for extra flavor and color.
- Serve hot as a side dish with roti, paratha, or rice.
