Lemon Drizzle Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (for the drizzle)
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice (for the drizzle)

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat the Oven
- Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan or line it with parchment paper.
Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients
- In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
Step 3: Cream Butter and Sugar
- In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. This should take about 3-4 minutes.
Step 4: Add Eggs and Lemon
- Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the lemon zest and lemon juice.
Step 5: Add Dry Ingredients
- Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Mix until just combined. Do not overmix.
Step 6: Pour into Cake Pan
- Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
Step 7: Bake the Cake
- Bake the cake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Step 8: Prepare the Lemon Drizzle
- While the cake is baking, prepare the drizzle by combining 1/4 cup sugar and 2 tbsp lemon juice in a small saucepan.
- Heat over low heat, stirring until the sugar dissolves completely. Remove from heat and set aside.
Step 9: Cool the Cake
- Allow the cake to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then, transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
Step 10: Drizzle the Lemon Syrup
- Once the cake has cooled, poke a few holes in the top of the cake using a skewer or fork.
- Pour the lemon drizzle over the cake, allowing it to soak in.
Step 11: Serve
- Let the cake sit for a few minutes to allow the drizzle to soak in before slicing and serving.
- Enjoy your tangy and refreshing lemon drizzle cake!
