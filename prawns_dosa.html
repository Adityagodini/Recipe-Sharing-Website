Prawns Dosa

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup dosa batter (or store-bought batter)
  • 250g prawns (peeled, deveined, and chopped)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 lemon (juice)
  • 1 tbsp curry leaves

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Prawn Filling
- Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
- Add onions and green chilies. Saut√© until the onions turn soft.
- Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
- Add prawns, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala powder, and salt.
- Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until prawns are cooked through.
- Squeeze in some lemon juice and mix in chopped coriander leaves. Set aside.
Step 2: Prepare the Dosa
- Heat a non-stick pan or tawa.
- Pour a ladleful of dosa batter onto the pan and spread it into a thin, round shape.
- Drizzle some oil around the edges and let it cook until the dosa turns crispy and golden brown.
Step 3: Add the Prawn Filling
- Once the dosa is cooked, spread a portion of the prawn filling over one half of the dosa.
Step 4: Roll the Dosa
- Fold the dosa over the prawn filling to form a roll.
Step 5: Serve
- Serve the Prawns Dosa hot with coconut chutney and sambar for dipping.
