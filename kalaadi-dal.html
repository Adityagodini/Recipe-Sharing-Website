Kalaadi Dal

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup kalaadi dal (black lentils)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1-2 cups water (adjust to desired consistency)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Kalaadi Dal
- Wash the kalaadi dal thoroughly. Soak it in water for about 30 minutes.
Step 2: Heat Oil and Temper Spices
- In a pot, heat oil. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and asafoetida. Let them splutter.
Step 3: Sauté Onions, Ginger, and Garlic
- Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
Step 4: Add Tomatoes and Spices
- Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, and salt.
- Cook until the tomatoes soften and the oil separates from the masala.
Step 5: Add Kalaadi Dal and Cook
- Add the soaked kalaadi dal to the pot. Stir well to combine with the spices.
Step 6: Simmer and Cook the Dal
- Add 1-2 cups of water depending on the consistency you prefer.
- Cover and cook the dal on low heat for 20-30 minutes or until the dal is soft and cooked through.
Step 7: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve the kalaadi dal hot with steamed rice or chapati.
