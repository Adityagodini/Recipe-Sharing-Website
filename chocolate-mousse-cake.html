Chocolate Mousse Cake

INGREDIENTS

    For the Cake Base:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup boiling water

    • For the Chocolate Mousse:

  • 200g dark chocolate (70% cocoa)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large egg whites
  • 2 tbsp sugar

    • For the Ganache:

  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Cake Base
- Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a 9-inch round cake pan.
- In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
- Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until combined.
- Gradually add the boiling water, mixing until smooth. The batter will be thin.
- Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
- Let the cake cool completely.
Step 2: Make the Chocolate Mousse
- Melt the dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl over simmering water or microwave in 30-second intervals until smooth.
- Let it cool slightly.
- In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream and powdered sugar together until soft peaks form.
- In another bowl, whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks form, then gradually add sugar to stabilize the mixture.
- Fold the melted chocolate into the whipped cream gently, then fold in the egg whites, ensuring no air is lost.
- The mousse should be smooth and airy.
Step 3: Make the Ganache
- Heat the heavy cream for the ganache in a small saucepan until just boiling.
- Pour the hot cream over the dark chocolate and let it sit for a minute. Stir until smooth and glossy.
- Allow to cool slightly.
Step 4: Assemble the Cake
- Once the cake has cooled, slice it horizontally into two layers.
- Place the first layer on a serving platter and spread a layer of chocolate mousse on top.
- Place the second layer of cake on top and cover the entire cake with the remaining mousse.
Step 5: Finish with Ganache
- Pour the ganache over the top of the mousse-covered cake, allowing it to drip down the sides.
- Refrigerate the cake for at least 2 hours to set the mousse and ganache.
Step 6: Serve
- Once set, slice and serve chilled. Optionally, garnish with chocolate shavings or whipped cream.
