Prawns Kofta Malai

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g prawns (cleaned, deveined, and finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves (finely chopped)
  • Oil for frying the koftas

    • For Gravy:

  • 1/2 cup fresh cream (malai)
  • 1 cup onion, tomato, and cashew paste
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1/2 cup water

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Kofta Mixture
- In a bowl, combine finely chopped prawns, mashed boiled potatoes, breadcrumbs.
- ginger-garlic paste, chopped green chilies, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
Step 2: Form Koftas
- Divide the mixture into small portions and roll them into smooth balls (koftas).
Step 3: Fry the Koftas
- Heat oil in a pan or deep fryer.
- Fry the koftas in batches on medium heat until they turn golden brown and crispy.
- Remove them from the oil and drain on paper towels.
Step 4: Prepare the Gravy
- Heat ghee or oil in a separate pan. Add cumin seeds and allow them to splutter.
- Then add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© until fragrant.
Step 5: Cook the Gravy Base
- Add the onion, tomato, and cashew paste to the pan and cook.
- until the oil separates from the mixture and it turns golden brown.
Step 6: Add Spices and Cream
- Add red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt.
- Stir well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Then, add fresh cream and mix well to make a smooth gravy.
Step 7: Add Water and Simmer
- Add water to adjust the consistency of the gravy.
- Bring it to a simmer for about 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
Step 8: Add Koftas to Gravy
- Carefully place the fried prawns koftas into the gravy and simmer for another 5-7 minutes.
- allowing the koftas to soak up the creamy sauce.
Step 9: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or steamed rice.
