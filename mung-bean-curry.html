Mung Bean Curry

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup moong beans (mung dal), soaked overnight and sprouted
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Water as required

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Moong Sprouts
- Soak the moong beans overnight in water. Drain the water and allow them to sprout for 1-2 days in a warm place.
Step 2: Temper the Spices
- Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
Step 3: SautÃ© the Aromatics
- Add asafoetida (hing), followed by chopped onions, green chili, ginger, and garlic. SautÃ© until onions turn golden brown.
Step 4: Add Tomatoes and Spices
- Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Stir in turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt.
- Let the spices cook for 2-3 minutes.
Step 5: Add Moong Sprouts
- Add the sprouted moong beans to the pan and mix well with the spices.
Step 6: Add Water and Cook
- Add enough water to create a curry consistency and bring it to a boil.
- Cover and cook for 10-12 minutes or until the moong sprouts are tender.
Step 7: Final Touches
- Add garam masala and mix well. Adjust the salt and spices to taste. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
Step 8: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
