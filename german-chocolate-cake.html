German Chocolate Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 oz German chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 cup sweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat the Oven
- Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
Step 2: Prepare the Cake Batter
- In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
- In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract.
- Melt the German chocolate in the hot water and stir to combine.
- Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until well combined.
- Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans.
Step 3: Bake the Cake
- Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
- Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
Step 4: Prepare the Coconut-Pecan Frosting
- In a medium saucepan, combine the evaporated milk, butter, and brown sugar over medium heat.
- Stir occasionally until the butter is melted and the sugar is dissolved.
- Add the coconut and pecans to the mixture, and continue to cook for another 10 minutes, stirring constantly until it thickens.
- Remove from heat and allow the frosting to cool to room temperature.
Step 5: Assemble the Cake
- Once the cakes have completely cooled, place one layer on a serving plate.
- Spread a layer of the coconut-pecan frosting on top of the first cake layer.
- Place the second cake layer on top and cover the entire cake with the remaining frosting.
Step 6: Serve
- Slice and serve your delicious German Chocolate Cake. Enjoy!
