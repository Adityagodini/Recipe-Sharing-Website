Boston Cream Pie

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 2 tbsp corn syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (for glaze)

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat the Oven
- Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8-inch round cake pan.
Step 2: Prepare the Cake Batter
- In a medium bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
- In a separate bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy (about 3 minutes).
- Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
- Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients.
- Mix until just combined.
Step 3: Bake the Cake
- Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top.
- Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
- Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
Step 4: Make the Pastry Cream
- In a saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it starts to simmer.
- In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and sugar until light and fluffy.
- Gradually add the hot milk to the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
- then return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over medium heat until thickened.
- Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract. Allow the pastry cream to cool before using it as a filling.
Step 5: Prepare the Chocolate Glaze
- In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it begins to simmer.
- Remove from heat and add the chocolate chips and corn syrup.
- Stir until the chocolate is fully melted and the glaze is smooth. Stir in vanilla extract and let it cool slightly.
Step 6: Assemble the Boston Cream Pie
- Slice the cooled cake in half horizontally to create two layers.
- Spread the cooled pastry cream over the bottom layer of cake.
- Place the second cake layer on top and gently press down.
Step 7: Add the Chocolate Glaze
- Pour the chocolate glaze over the top of the cake, allowing it to drip down the sides.
- Let the cake sit for about 10-15 minutes to allow the glaze to set.
Step 8: Serve
- Slice and serve your delicious Boston Cream Pie. Enjoy!
