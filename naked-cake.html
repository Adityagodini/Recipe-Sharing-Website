Naked Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ¾ cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • Fresh whipped cream or buttercream (for layering)
  • Fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Fresh edible flowers or herbs (for decoration)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Cake
- Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and line 2 or 3 round cake pans.
- In a bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
- Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
- Beat in eggs one at a time, followed by vanilla extract.
- Add flour mixture and milk alternately, mixing until just combined.
Step 2: Bake
- Divide the batter evenly among the pans.
- Bake for 25–30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
- Let cakes cool completely on wire racks.
Step 3: Assemble Naked Style
- Trim the tops of cakes to make flat layers if needed.
- Place one cake layer on a stand. Add a thick layer of whipped cream or buttercream.
- Add fresh berries, then place the next layer on top. Repeat for all layers.
Step 4: Finishing Touch
- Apply a *thin scrape* of cream on the outside edges—let the cake peek through!
- Top with berries and decorate with edible flowers or sprigs of rosemary/mint.
Step 5: Chill and Serve
- Chill for 30 minutes before slicing. Serve slightly cold or room temp for best flavor.
