No Bake Blueberry Cheesecake

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g digestive biscuits (or graham crackers)
  • 75g melted butter
  • 300g cream cheese (softened)
  • 200ml whipping cream (chilled)
  • 100g powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 150g blueberry compote or fresh blueberry jam
  • Fresh blueberries (for garnish, optional)






Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Base
- Crush the biscuits and mix with melted butter.
- Press the mixture into a springform pan and refrigerate for 15â€“20 minutes.
Step 2: Make the Filling
- In a bowl, beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
- In a separate bowl, whip the chilled cream until stiff peaks form,
- then fold it into the cream cheese mixture.
Step 3: Assemble
- Pour and spread the cream filling over the chilled crust evenly.
- Smooth the surface using a spatula.
Step 4: Chill
- Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 6 hours or overnight until firm.
Step 5: Add Blueberry Topping
- Spread blueberry compote or jam on top of the cheesecake.
- Garnish with fresh blueberries if desired.
Step 6: Serve
- Carefully remove from the pan, slice, and serve chilled.
