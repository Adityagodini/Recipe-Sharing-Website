Malai Panner

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 cup fresh cream (malai or heavy cream)
  • 2 onions (boiled and pureed)
  • 1/2 cup cashew paste (soaked & ground cashews)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper powder
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp ghee or butter
  • Fresh coriander or cream for garnish

Recipe

Step 1: Sauté Whole Spices
- Heat ghee in a pan. Add bay leaf and cumin seeds. Let them crackle.
Step 2: Prepare the Gravy Base
- Add boiled onion puree and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
- Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2–3 minutes.
- Add the cashew paste, mix well and cook on low flame.
Step 3: Add Cream and Spices
- Add fresh cream, white pepper, cardamom powder, and salt. Stir gently.
- Add a splash of water if needed to adjust consistency.
Step 4: Add Paneer
- Add paneer cubes and simmer for 4–5 minutes on low flame.
- Sprinkle garam masala and give a gentle mix.
Step 5: Garnish & Serve
- Garnish with a swirl of cream and chopped coriander.
- Serve hot with butter naan, jeera rice, or laccha paratha.
