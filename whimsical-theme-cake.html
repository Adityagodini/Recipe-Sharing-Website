Whimsical Theme Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • Buttercream frosting (in various colors for decoration)
  • Fondant for decoration (optional)
  • Edible glitter, sprinkles, and colorful sugar decorations
  • Miniature fondant shapes (stars, hearts, clouds, etc.)
  • Food coloring for vibrant colors

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat & Prepare
- Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and line your cake pans with parchment paper.
Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients
- In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
Step 3: Cream Butter & Sugar
- In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until the mixture becomes light and fluffy.
Step 4: Add Eggs & Vanilla
- Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
Step 5: Alternate Adding Dry Ingredients & Milk
- Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with the milk.
- Start and end with the dry ingredients. Mix until smooth.
Step 6: Bake the Cake
- Divide the batter into separate bowls and color each one with different food coloring.
- Pour each colored batter into the prepared cake pans and bake for 25-30 minutes.
- until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
- Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
Step 7: Frost the Cake
- Once the cakes have cooled, stack the layers on a cake stand or plate.
- Apply a thin layer of buttercream frosting between each layer and around the edges.
Step 8: Decorate with Fondant & Whimsical Designs
- Roll out fondant and create whimsical decorations like stars, hearts, clouds, rainbows, or animals.
- Place them on the cake creatively to give it a fun, magical look.
Step 9: Final Touches
- Apply edible glitter, sprinkles, and colorful sugar decorations for a playful effect.
- Add miniature fondant characters or objects to enhance the whimsical theme.
Step 10: Serve
- Slice and serve your whimsical theme cake! It’s sure to be a hit at parties or special celebrations with its colorful and fun design.
