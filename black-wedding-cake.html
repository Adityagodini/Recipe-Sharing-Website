Black Wedding Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 ½ cups unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp black food coloring
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • Black buttercream frosting
  • Gold leaf (optional, for decoration)
  • Edible pearls (optional)
  • Fondant (for decorations, optional)

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat & Prepare
- Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and line your cake pans with parchment paper.
Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients
- In a large bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and sugar. Set aside.
Step 3: Combine Wet Ingredients
- In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, vegetable oil, eggs, vanilla extract, and black food coloring until smooth.
Step 4: Mix Wet and Dry Ingredients
- Slowly add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients. Stir until fully combined.
Step 5: Add Boiling Water
- Gradually add the boiling water to the batter, mixing gently. The batter will be thin, which is normal.
Step 6: Bake the Cake
- Pour the batter into the prepared cake pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
- until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
- Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
Step 7: Frost the Cake
- Once the cakes are completely cooled, frost with black buttercream frosting.
- Apply a crumb coat first, refrigerate for 30 minutes, then apply a final smooth layer of frosting.
Step 8: Decorate with Gold & Edible Pearls
- Add edible gold leaf to the sides or top of the cake for an elegant touch.
- You can also decorate with edible pearls for a luxurious finish.
Step 9: Fondant Decorations
- If desired, use black fondant to create elegant decorations such as flowers, lace patterns, or geometric designs.
- Apply these decorations to the cake for added flair.
Step 10: Serve
- Slice and serve the cake. The striking black color combined with the luxurious gold accents makes this wedding cake a showstopper.
