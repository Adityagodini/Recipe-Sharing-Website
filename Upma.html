Step 1: Roast Sooji
- Dry roast the rava in a pan on medium heat until aromatic. Keep aside.
Step 2: Tempering
- Heat oil/ghee, add mustard seeds, let them splutter. Add urad dal, chana dal, and fry till golden.
Step 3: Saut√©
- Add chopped onions, green chilies, curry leaves and saut√© until onions turn translucent.
Step 4: Add Water
- Pour in water, add salt. Bring it to a boil.
Step 5: Add Rava
- Slowly add roasted rava, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Cook until water is absorbed and upma is soft.
Step 6: Garnish
- Add chopped coriander leaves and a few drops of ghee if desired. Mix well and turn off flame.

Tomato Chutney

Ingredients:
- 2 ripe tomatoes, 2 dried red chilies, 1 garlic clove, salt, tamarind (small piece)
Step 1
- Heat 1 tsp oil, fry red chilies and garlic for a few seconds.
Step 2
- Add chopped tomatoes and tamarind, cook till tomatoes turn mushy.
Step 3
- Cool and blend to a smooth paste with salt. Optionally temper with mustard, curry leaves and urad dal.
