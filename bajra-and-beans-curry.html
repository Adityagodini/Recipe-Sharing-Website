Bajra and Beans Curry

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup bajra (pearl millet)
  • 1/2 cup kidney beans (or your choice of beans, soaked overnight)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 3-4 cups water (adjust for consistency)

Recipe

Step 1: Cook Bajra and Beans
- In a pressure cooker, cook the bajra and soaked beans with 3-4 cups of water.
- Cook for about 4-5 whistles or until both the bajra and beans are soft and cooked through. Set aside.
Step 2: Temper the Spices
- In a pan, heat ghee or oil. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
Step 3: Sauté the Aromatics
- Add the chopped onions, green chilies, grated ginger, and minced garlic.
- Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.
Step 4: Add Tomatoes and Spices
- Add the chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala.
- Cook until the tomatoes turn soft and oil separates from the masala.
Step 5: Combine the Bajra and Beans
- Add the cooked bajra and beans mixture to the pan. Mix well and add salt to taste.
- Add water to reach the desired curry consistency.
Step 6: Simmer the Curry
- Let the curry simmer on medium heat for about 10-15 minutes so the flavors can blend together.
Step 7: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with roti, rice, or a side of your choice.
