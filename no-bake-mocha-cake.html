No Bake Mocha Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups crushed graham crackers
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 package (8 oz) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/4 cup instant coffee or espresso powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (for topping)





Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Crust
- In a bowl, combine the crushed graham crackers, cocoa powder, and melted butter. Mix until fully combined.
- Press this mixture into the bottom of a springform pan or pie dish to form the crust.
- Chill in the refrigerator while you prepare the filling.
Step 2: Prepare the Mocha Filling
- In a mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese with the powdered sugar until smooth.
- In a separate small bowl, dissolve the instant coffee or espresso powder in 1 tablespoon of warm water.
- Add this coffee mixture, heavy cream, and vanilla extract to the cream cheese mixture, and beat until smooth and creamy.
Step 3: Assemble the Cake
- Spread the mocha filling evenly over the prepared graham cracker crust.
- Smooth the top with a spatula.
- Refrigerate the cake for at least 4 hours or until set.
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
- Before serving, sprinkle the top with chocolate chips. Slice and enjoy your no-bake mocha cake!
