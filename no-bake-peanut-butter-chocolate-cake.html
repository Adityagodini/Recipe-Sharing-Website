No Bake Peanut Butter Chocolate Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g digestive biscuits or graham crackers (crushed)
  • 100g melted butter
  • 200g smooth peanut butter
  • 100g powdered sugar
  • 200g dark or milk chocolate (chopped)
  • 100ml heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Crushed peanuts or chocolate chips (for garnish, optional)





Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Base
- Mix crushed biscuits with melted butter and press into a lined cake tin.
- Refrigerate for 20 minutes to set.
Step 2: Make Peanut Butter Layer
- Combine peanut butter, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.
- Spread this over the chilled base and return to the fridge for 30 minutes.
Step 3: Make Chocolate Ganache
- Heat the cream until just simmering, then pour over chopped chocolate.
- Let sit for 2â€“3 minutes, then stir until smooth and glossy.
Step 4: Assemble
- Pour the chocolate ganache over the peanut butter layer.
- Smooth the top and garnish with crushed peanuts or chocolate chips if desired.
Step 5: Chill and Serve
- Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until fully set. Slice and serve chilled.
