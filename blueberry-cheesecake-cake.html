Blueberry Cheesecake

INGREDIENTS

    For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk

    • For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 2 cups cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 eggs

    • For the Blueberry Sauce:

  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch (optional, for thickening)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Cake
- Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C) and grease two 9-inch round cake pans.
- In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
- In a separate bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
- Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition, then stir in the vanilla extract.
- Gradually add the dry ingredients and milk alternately, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients.
- Mix until smooth and combined.
- Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans and bake for 25-30 minutes .
- until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow cakes to cool completely.
Step 2: Make the Cheesecake Filling
- In a bowl, beat the cream cheese and powdered sugar together until smooth and creamy.
- Add vanilla extract, sour cream, and lemon juice, then beat again until fully combined.
- Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
- Pour the cheesecake mixture over the cooled cake layers, spreading it evenly.
- Bake at 350째F (175째C) for about 45 minutes, or until the cheesecake is set and the top is slightly golden.
- Let it cool completely in the pan.
Step 3: Prepare the Blueberry Sauce
- In a saucepan, combine blueberries, sugar, and lemon juice.
- Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
- until the blueberries release their juices and the mixture thickens slightly.
- If you prefer a thicker sauce, mix cornstarch with a little water and add it to the sauce while cooking,
- stirring until the sauce thickens further.
- Remove from heat and let it cool to room temperature.
Step 4: Assemble the Cake
- Once the cheesecake filling has cooled, layer the cake and cheesecake.
- Place one layer of cake on a serving platter, then top it with a generous layer of blueberry sauce.
- Place the second cake layer on top, pressing gently.
- Spread more blueberry sauce over the top, allowing it to drizzle down the sides.
Step 5: Chill and Serve
- Chill the cake in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours to set the flavors and textures.
- Serve cold, garnished with extra blueberries or whipped cream, if desired.
