Gongura Dal

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 1.5 cups gongura leaves (sorrel), washed and chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water (for pressure cooking)
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee

Recipe

Step 1: Cook Dal
- Wash toor dal and pressure cook it with turmeric, chopped onions, green chilies, and 2 cups of water for 3‚Äì4 whistles.
Step 2: Prepare Gongura
- In a separate pan, heat 1 tbsp oil and saut√© chopped gongura leaves until soft and wilted. Set aside.
Step 3: Mash & Mix
- Once the dal is cool, mash it slightly and add the cooked gongura to it. Mix well and bring to a gentle boil. Add salt as needed.
Step 4: Tempering
- Heat oil or ghee in a small pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin, red chilies, garlic, hing, and curry leaves. Let it crackle.
Step 5: Final Touch
- Pour the tempering over the gongura dal and mix gently. Let it simmer for a minute for the flavors to blend.
Step 6: Serve
- Serve hot with steamed rice, ghee, and a side of fryums or papad. Classic Andhra comfort food!
