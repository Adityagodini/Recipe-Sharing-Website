Tinda Masala

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups Tinda (Apple gourd), peeled and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 large tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • 1/4 cup water (optional)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Tinda
- Peel and slice the Tinda into round pieces.
- Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
- Add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
Step 2: Cook the Spices
- Add ginger-garlic paste to the onions and sauté for a minute.
- Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder.
- Cook until the tomatoes soften and the oil starts to separate.
Step 3: Cook the Tinda
- Add the sliced Tinda to the pan and mix well to coat the pieces with the masala.
- Add salt to taste and stir to combine. Cook for 5 minutes on medium heat.
- Cover the pan and let the Tinda cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
- If needed, add a little water to prevent sticking.
Step 4: Finish and Garnish
- Once the Tinda is cooked and tender, add garam masala and stir well.
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.
