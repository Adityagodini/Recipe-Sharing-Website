Semi-Naked cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 ¾ cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • Buttercream frosting (vanilla, chocolate, or cream cheese-based)
  • Fresh fruits (berries, figs, citrus slices)
  • Edible flowers or gold leaf (optional, for decoration)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Cake Batter
- Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and line three 6" or 8" round cake pans.
- Sift flour, baking powder, and salt together.
- Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
- Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla extract.
- Alternate adding dry ingredients and milk, mixing until smooth.
Step 2: Bake
- Pour batter evenly into pans.
- Bake for 25–30 minutes or until golden and springy to touch.
- Cool completely before frosting.
Step 3: Stack the Cake
- Level cake layers if needed.
- Place one layer on a cake stand. Add a layer of frosting and spread evenly.
- Stack all layers with frosting in between.
Step 4: Semi-Naked Frosting
- Apply a thin crumb coat of frosting around the sides using an offset spatula.
- Scrape off excess to let some cake peek through for the “semi-naked” effect.
- Chill for 20 minutes to set the crumb coat.
Step 5: Decorate
- Top with fresh fruits, flowers, or a light dusting of powdered sugar.
- Add edible gold leaf for an elegant finish if desired.
Step 6: Serve
- Slice with a hot knife for clean cuts. Serve and wow your guests!
