Chilli Panner

INGREDIENTS

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1/2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp chili sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp green onion (chopped, for garnish)
  • Salt (to taste)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Paneer
- In a bowl, mix the cornflour, all-purpose flour, salt, and red chili powder.
- Coat the paneer cubes with the flour mixture, ensuring they are evenly covered.
- Heat oil in a pan and fry the paneer cubes until they are golden and crisp. Set aside.
Step 2: Prepare the Sauce
- In the same pan, add a bit of oil and saut√© the ginger-garlic paste until fragrant.
- Add the sliced onions, bell peppers, and slit green chilies. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
- Add the soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, sugar, and a pinch of salt. Stir well.
Step 3: Combine the Paneer with Sauce
- Once the sauce has thickened slightly, add the fried paneer cubes to the pan.
- Toss the paneer in the sauce to coat it well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
Step 4: Garnish & Serve
- Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot as a starter or side dish.
