No Bake Strawberry Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g digestive biscuits (or graham crackers)
  • 75g melted butter
  • 250g cream cheese (softened)
  • 200ml whipping cream (chilled)
  • 150g fresh strawberry puree
  • 100g powdered sugar
  • 2 tsp gelatin (or agar-agar for vegetarian)
  • 2 tbsp warm water (to dissolve gelatin)
  • Fresh strawberries for garnish





Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Crust
- Crush the biscuits into fine crumbs and mix with melted butter.
- Press into the base of a springform pan and refrigerate for 15 minutes.
Step 2: Make the Filling
- Whip the chilled cream until soft peaks form.
- In another bowl, blend cream cheese, strawberry puree, and powdered sugar until smooth.
Step 3: Add Gelatin
- Dissolve gelatin in warm water and mix into the strawberry mixture.
- Fold in the whipped cream gently.
Step 4: Set the Cake
- Pour the filling over the chilled crust, level the top.
- and refrigerate for at least 6 hours or overnight.
Step 5: Garnish and Serve
- Decorate with fresh strawberries on top. Slice and serve chilled!
