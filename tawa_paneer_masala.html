Tawa Panner Masala

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 large tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 green bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1 red bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/4 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp fresh coriander leaves (chopped, for garnish)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)

Recipe

Step 1: Heat Oil and Temper Spices
- Heat oil or ghee in a tawa or wide pan over medium heat.
- Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Then add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant.
Step 2: Cook Onions and Peppers
- Add finely chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
- Add chopped green and red bell peppers, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until they slightly soften.
Step 3: Add Tomatoes and Dry Spices
- Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and the oil separates from the masala.
- Stir in turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and garam masala powder. Mix well.
Step 4: Add Paneer
- Add the cubed paneer pieces to the tawa and gently mix with the masala.
- Cook for 4-5 minutes, stirring occasionally, allowing the paneer to absorb the flavors.
Step 5: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
- Optionally, squeeze lemon juice for an added tanginess.
- Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.
