Macaron Wedding Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • Buttercream frosting (vanilla or any color of your choice)
  • Sprinkles (optional)
  • Decorative toppers (flowers, edible glitter, etc.) for the tower

Recipe

Step 1: Preheat & Prepare the Cupcake Pan
- Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Line cupcake pans with cupcake liners or grease them lightly.
Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients
- In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
Step 3: Cream Butter & Sugar
- In a large bowl, beat the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy, about 3-5 minutes.
- You can use a hand mixer or stand mixer for this.
Step 4: Add Eggs & Vanilla
- Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
Step 5: Combine Wet & Dry Ingredients
- Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with milk.
- beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until smooth.
Step 6: Fill Cupcake Pans
- Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
Step 7: Bake the Cupcakes
- Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.
- Let the cupcakes cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
Step 8: Prepare Buttercream Frosting
- While the cupcakes are cooling, prepare the buttercream frosting.
- In a large bowl, beat 1 cup of softened butter and 4 cups of powdered sugar until smooth.
- Add 1-2 tsp vanilla extract and a few tablespoons of milk to achieve the desired consistency.
Step 9: Frost the Cupcakes
- Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, frost each cupcake generously with the buttercream frosting.
- You can use a piping bag with your favorite tip for a decorative effect.
Step 10: Assemble the Cupcake Tower
- Arrange the frosted cupcakes in a tiered fashion on a cupcake tower stand or a cake stand.
- You can use dowels or cake boards to ensure each layer of the tower is stable.
Step 11: Decorate the Tower
- Add decorative elements like sprinkles, edible flowers.
- any themed toppers to the cupcakes and tower for an extra touch of elegance.
Step 12: Serve the Cupcake Tower Cake
- Serve your beautiful cupcake tower cake as the centerpiece of your event.
- Each guest can enjoy a cupcake from the tower, making it a fun and festive dessert option!
