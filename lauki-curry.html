Lauki Curry

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups Lauki (Bottle gourd), peeled and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup water (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Lauki
- Peel and chop the Lauki into cubes.
- Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
- Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
Step 2: Cook the Spices
- Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
- Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder.
- Cook until the tomatoes turn soft and the oil separates from the masala.
Step 3: Cook the Lauki
- Add the chopped Lauki to the pan and mix well to coat with the spices.
- Add salt to taste and mix again.
- Cover the pan and cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add a little water if required to prevent the curry from sticking.
Step 4: Finish and Garnish
- Once the Lauki is tender, sprinkle garam masala and stir well.
- Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
- Serve hot with roti, rice, or paratha.
