Lal Saag

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 bunches Lal Saag (red amaranth leaves), chopped
  • 1 medium onion, finely sliced
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 dry red chili
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil or any oil of choice
  • Salt to taste

Recipe

Step 1: Prep the Saag
- Wash the lal saag thoroughly in water to remove any mud. Drain and chop finely.
Step 2: Temper the Oil
- Heat mustard oil in a pan until it smokes lightly. Add mustard seeds and dry red chili. Let them splutter.
Step 3: Sauté Aromatics
- Add garlic and green chili. Sauté for a minute. Then add sliced onions and cook until translucent.
Step 4: Add Lal Saag
- Add the chopped lal saag, sprinkle salt, and mix well.
- Cook uncovered on medium flame for 5–7 minutes until the saag wilts and moisture evaporates.
Step 5: Serve
- Serve hot with steamed rice and dal for a wholesome meal.
