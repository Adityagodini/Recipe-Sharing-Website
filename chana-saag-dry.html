Chana Saag Dry

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas (chana)
  • 2 cups chopped spinach (palak) or mixed saag
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Recipe

Step 1: Heat Oil & Temper
- Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Then add garlic and green chili, sauté for a few seconds.
Step 2: Cook Onions & Masala
- Add chopped onions and sauté till golden. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala. Mix well.
Step 3: Add Spinach
- Add the chopped spinach and cook for 3–4 minutes until wilted and soft.
Step 4: Add Chickpeas
- Add boiled chickpeas and mix with the saag. Cook for 5–7 minutes till flavors blend and water evaporates.
Step 5: Serve
- Serve hot with roti, paratha, or a side of rice and dal. Nutritious and satisfying!
