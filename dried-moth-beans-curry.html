Dried Mothi Beans Curry

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup dried moth beans (moth dal)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Water for cooking

Recipe

Step 1: Soak and Cook the Moth Beans
- Wash the dried moth beans thoroughly and soak them in water for 6-8 hours or overnight.
- Drain the water and pressure cook the soaked moth beans with 3 cups of water for about 4-5 whistles or until they are soft. Set aside.
Step 2: Prepare the Tempering
- Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
Step 3: Add Onions, Garlic, and Ginger
- Add chopped onions, green chili, garlic, and ginger. SautÃ© until the onions turn golden brown.
Step 4: Add Tomatoes and Spices
- Add chopped tomatoes to the pan and cook until they soften.
- Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Stir well and cook until the oil starts to separate from the masala.
Step 5: Combine Moth Beans with Masala
- Add the cooked moth beans (with any remaining cooking water) to the pan. Stir well to combine the beans with the masala.
Step 6: Cook the Moth Beans Curry
- Let the moth beans cook on medium heat for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
- Add water if necessary to adjust the consistency of the curry.
Step 7: Garnish and Serve
- Once the curry thickens and the beans are well coated in the spices, garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
