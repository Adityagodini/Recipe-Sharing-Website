Methi Thepla Wrap

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup fresh methi (fenugreek) leaves, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil (for dough + more for roasting)

Recipe

Step 1: Make Dough
- In a mixing bowl, combine wheat flour, chopped methi leaves, curd, ginger-chili paste,
- turmeric, red chili powder, cumin seeds, salt, and 2 tbsp oil.
- Knead into a soft, pliable dough using little water if needed. Rest for 15 minutes.
Step 2: Roll and Stuff
- Divide the dough into equal-sized balls.
- Roll out each into a small circle, add any stuffing of choice (paneer bhurji,
- mashed spiced potatoes, etc.), seal and gently roll again.
Step 3: Roast the Theplas
- Heat a tawa, place the rolled thepla and roast on both sides with little oil until golden brown and crispy.
Step 4: Serve Hot
- Serve warm with pickle, curd, or chutney of your choice.
