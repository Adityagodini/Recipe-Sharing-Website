Lobia Fry

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup black-eyed peas (lobia), soaked overnight
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1/2 cup water

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Black-eyed Peas
- Soak the lobia (black-eyed peas) overnight in water. Drain them before cooking.
Step 2: Heat Oil and Temper Spices
- In a pan, heat oil. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and asafoetida. Let them splutter.
Step 3: Add Onions, Ginger, and Garlic
- Add chopped onions, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Saut√© until the onions turn golden brown.
Step 4: Add Tomatoes and Spices
- Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
- Cook until the tomatoes become soft and the oil separates.
Step 5: Add Black-eyed Peas
- Add the soaked and drained black-eyed peas to the pan. Mix well to coat them in the spices.
Step 6: Cook and Stir-fry
- Add 1/2 cup water, cover the pan, and cook on low heat for 10-15 minutes. Stir occasionally to prevent burning.
Step 7: Garnish and Serve
- Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with chapati or rice.
