Eggless Coffee Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp instant coffee powder
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1/2 cup oil or melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
  • Optional: nuts or chocolate chips for topping


Recipe

Step 1: Preheat Oven
- Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake tin or loaf pan.
Step 2: Mix Wet Ingredients
- In a bowl, mix milk, vinegar (or lemon juice), coffee powder, oil, and vanilla extract.
- Let it sit for 5 minutes to curdle slightly.
Step 3: Combine Dry Ingredients
- In another bowl, combine flour, sugar, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
Step 4: Make the Batter
- Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Stir until just combined.
- Do not overmix.
Step 5: Bake
- Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Sprinkle nuts or chocolate chips on top if using.
- Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
Step 6: Cool and Serve
- Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
- Slice and enjoy with your favorite beverage.
